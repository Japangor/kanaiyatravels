Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI - VARANSI
   Depart For Varanasi By Train from Mumbai Cst OR Ltt Terminal. at Morning. Overnight Train 
Day 02 :    ARRIVE VARANSI
   Arrive Varanasi at afternoon  Transfer to hotel, After refreshments, Proceed to visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Golden Temple). Later go to Dashaswamedh Ghat and experience Evening Ganga Aarti one of the most memorable moments. Return back to Hotel. Overnight Varanasi.
Day 03 :    VARANSI (251 KM)
   Early morning by 5:30am move to Assi Ghat to experience the memorable morning. See all the ghats on the bank of Ganges river by Boat (At Your Own Cost) and witness the rising sun. Back to hotel for breakfast and rest for an hour. By 10am visit Bharat Mata (Mother India) Mandir, Durga, Tulsi Manas, Sankat Mochan Temple and the new Vishwanath Temple Kal Bhairava Tempel and Birla Temple. After late Lunch Visit Sarnath. Overnight Varanasi.
Day 04 :    VARANSI - BODH GAYA( 251 KM)
   After breakfast, Drive to Bodhgaya (265km/6hrs) Lunch on the way. On arrival check in to the hotel. See 80 feet giant statue of Buddha, The main Mahabodhi temple in Bodhgaya where Buddha meditated and attained enlightenment under the bodhi tree. Visit other international temples of Buddha attraction. Night halt in Bodhgaya.
Day 05 :    BODH GAYA - VARANSI (251 KM)
   Early morning by 5:30am proceed to Gaya (15km/30min) and have darshan and perform Pitru Tarpan Pooja (Pind Daan) at Vishunpadam Temple, Purohit's may take you to Phalgu River for rituals. Later drive back to Bodhgaya for breakfast. After Breakfast check out from hotel and drive to Varanasi. Arrive at Evening, Rest of Day free time for Shopping from Godowlia Market (Famous for Banarsi Saree) Overnight Varansi.
Day 06 :    VARANSI - CHAPPAIYA - AYODHYA (285 KM)
   After and Early Breakfast Check -Out From Hotel & drive to Chappaiya after arrive visit temple for Swaminarayan Birth Place, Later proceed to Ayodhya On arrival check into the hotel, Evening visit to Rama Janmbhumi – the birth place of lord Rama and other temples. Overnight Ayodhya. 
Day 07 :    AYODHYA - ALLAHABAD (175 KM)
   After Breakfast Check -Out From Hotel & procced for Allahabad , After Arrive transfer to Hotel. After lunch visit Sangam for a holi dip and later visit other places of religious and historical importance. Overnight Allahabad. 
Day 08 :    ALLAHABAD - CHITRKOOT - ALLAHABAD (258 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast at hotel drive to Chitrakoot (Madhya Pradesh). According to Hindu Mythology Lord Rama, Sita and Lakshmana spent eleven and half years of their fourteen years of exile. Visit Ramghat, Kamadgiri, Bharat Milap, Janaki Kund, etc. Later return to Allahabad and Overnight Allahabad. 
Day 09 :    ALLAHABAD - MUMBAI (BY TRAIN
   After Lunch Check -Out From Hotel and drop to Railway Station OR Airport to Board your train for your Return Journey to Mumbai. Overnight Train.
Day 10 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai at Evening. Tour end with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passengers travelling by air has to reach Banglore airport at their own expense. Tour END
