Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    JAMMU / UDHAMPUR RAILWAY STATION TO PAHELGAM (290 KM) OR SRINAGAR TO PAHELGAM (90 KM)
   Pick Up From Jammu Railway Station and Proceed for Pahelgam (275 KM & 8 To 9 Hrs), OR Pick Up From Srinagar Airport and Drive to Pahelgam (2 Hrs). Transfer to Hotel. Evening Free time for Shopping. Overnight Pahelgam.
Day 02 :    PAHELGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley (at your Own cost) the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley. Enjoy horse riding at this beautiful place, Evening is free Time for shopping Overnight Pahalgam..Overnight Pahelgam.
Day 03 :    PAHELGAM - SAFRON FIRM - AVANTIPORE - GULMARG (140 KM)
   After breakfast proceed to Gulmarg (5 hrs. journey ).(9,500 ft. above sea level). Enroute visit Safron Firm, Cricket Bat Factory, Avantipora Museam,Maturn Tirth & also Enroute pick up overcoats, gloves and boots, available on hire (optional), at Tangmarg. After lunch , the day is free Time for Horse Riding and Shopping .Overnight Gulmarg.
Day 04 :    GULMARG
   In the morning a walk upto Gandola Cable Car Station (1 To 2 Km) to enjoy Gandola Cable Car Ride (at Your Own Cost) & enjoy snow at this beautiful snow point. rest of the day is free to enjoy photography, horse riding. ( please be aware, here you can be cheated by horse riders). Overnight Gulmarg. 
Day 05 :    GULMARG - SRINAGAR (90 KM)
   After breakfast proceed to Srinagar. Enroute visit & Shopping .Apple & Dryfruit Firm. Rest Of Day free time for Shopping & Sikara Ride. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 06 :    SRINAGAR - SONMARG - KHIR BHAWANI - SRINAGAR  ( 170 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast full day trip to Sonmarg, which is located at 90 kms from Srinagar at 2740 mtrs. above Sea level. Sonmarg means meadow of gold. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point (at your own cost). Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass / Thajiwas By Local Texi (at your own cost - Subject to Military Permission) Evening back to Srinagar. Enroute visit Khir Bhavani Temple. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 07 :    SRINAGAR LOCAL
   Early morning visit Sri Shankaracharya Temple.. (1100 ft. above surface level of the main city on the hill, Climb 270 steps to reach the temple). from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Srinagar. After breakfast visit Moghul Gardens i.e. Chashmeshahi - Nishat Baug - and Shalimar . Enjoy Shikara Ride . (At Your Own Coast). covering Nehru Park, Charchinar and Floating Market. . Overnight Srinagar.
Day 08 :    DROP TO UDHAMPUR / JAMMU / KATRA RAILWAY STATION (8 To 9 Hrs) OR DROP TO SRINAGAR AIRPORT
   After breakfast Drop To Srinagar Airport (25 km & 1 Hrs) to board Your Flight For your return journey. OR Drop to Udhampur / Katra / Jammu Railway Station (250 KM & 8 Hrs). TOUR END with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
