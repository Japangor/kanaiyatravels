Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    JAMMU / UDHAMPUR RAILWAY STATION TO PAHELGAM (258 KM) OR SRINAGAR TO PAHELGAM (90 KM)
   Pick Up From Jammu Railway Station and Proceed for Pahelgam (250 KM & 8 To 8 Hrs), OR Pick Up From Srinagar Airport and Drive to Pahelgam (2 Hrs). Transfer to Hotel. Evening Free time for Shopping. Overnight Pahelgam.
Day 02 :    PAHELGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley (Include in Tour cost) the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley. Enjoy horse riding at this beautiful place, Evening is free Time for shopping Overnight Pahalgam..Overnight Pahelgam.
Day 03 :    PAHELGAM - SAFRON FIRM - AVANTIPORE - GULMARG (140 KM)
   After breakfast proceed to Gulmarg (5 hrs. journey ).(9,500 ft. above sea level). Enroute visit Safron Firm, Cricket Bat Factory, Avantipora Museam,Maturn Tirth & also Enroute pick up overcoats, gloves and boots, available on hire (optional), at Tangmarg. After lunch , the day is free Time for Horse Riding and Shopping .Overnight Gulmarg.
Day 04 :    GULMARG
   In the morning a walk upto Gandola Cable Car Station (1 To 2 Km) to enjoy Gandola Cable Car Ride (at Your Own Cost) & enjoy snow at this beautiful snow point. rest of the day is free to enjoy photography, horse riding. ( please be aware, here you can be cheated by horse riders). Overnight Gulmarg. 
Day 05 :    GULMARG - SONMARG (150 KM)
   After breakfast proceed to Sonmarg(4-5 Hrs). Sonmarg at 2,740 mtrs.above sea level.Sonmarg which means "meadow of Gold"is a picturesque location. Arrive Sonmarg and check-in at hotel, After Lunch enjoy sledge car ride at this beautiful snow point, Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass (Subject to Military Permission) Or Thaziwas Glaciar ( By Local Texi .at your own cost),. Overnight Sonmarg.
Day 06 :    SONMARG - SRINAGAR (85 KM)
   Morning to Afternoon Free time for Enjoy Natural Beauty, Photographi, Horse Riding. After Lunch proceed for Srinagar (3 Hrs), Enroute Can intresed are Enjoy River Rafting, and also visit Khirbhavani Temple. Arrive Srinagar at Evening Overnight Srinagar. 
Day 07 :    SRINAGAR LOCAL
   Early morning visit Shankaracharya Temple. (1100 ft. above surface level of the main city on the hill, Climb 270 steps to reach the temple). from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Srinagar. After breakfast visit Moghul Gardens i.e. Chashmeshahi - Nishat Baug - and Shalimar. Enjoy Shikara Ride . (At Your Own Coast). covering Nehru Park, Charchinar and Floating Market. . Overnight Srinagar.
Day 08 :    DROP TO SRINAGAR AIRPORT (20 KM) OR DROP UDHAMPUR RAILWAY STATION (200 KM) OR DROP TO JAMMU RAILWAY STATION (258 KM)
   After breakfast we proceed to Udhampur Railway Station (8-9 Hrs) OR Jammu station (10 -11 Hrs. journey) to board Your Train. with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passengers travelling by air has Drop to Srinagar airport.(1 Hrs).  TOUR END
