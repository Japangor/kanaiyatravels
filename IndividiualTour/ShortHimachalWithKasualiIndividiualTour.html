Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    AMBALA / CHANDIGARH TO PINJORE TO SHIMLA  (4 Hrs) (ONLY DINNER)
   Arrive on Ambala / Chandigarh Railway Station / Airport & drive to Shimla. Enroute visit Pinjore Garden. Arrive shimla & transfer to Hotel. (Shimla the state capital of Himachal Pradesh is surrounded by pine deodar, oak and rhododendron forests. Shimla was also the summer capital of the British Raj) Night halt at Shimla.
Day 02 :    SHIMLA TO KUFRI TO SHIMLA  (70 KM RETURN) (BREAKFAST & DINNER)
   After breakfast visit Kufri, Mini Zoo and enjoy horse riding in the Apple Orchids . After lunch Evening visit to the Mall, stately mansions, and curiosity shops.Overnight Shimla.
Day 03 :    SHIMLA TO KASAULI  (70 KM / 3 - 4 Hrs) (BREAKFAST & DINNER)
   After morning breakfast we will travel by toy train from Shimla to Kandaghat. Enjoy the Cable car in Timber trail. On the way to Kasauli, we will move towards Kasauli seeing the beautiful view of Parwanoo Valley and Dharampur Valley. Arrive at afternoon and transfar in hotel. After fresh Up visit Monkey Point (Monkey Point is the highest peak of the town of Kasauli), Baptist Church & local small Mall Road of kasauli. Night Halt in Kasauli OR Dharampur.
Day 04 :    KASAULI TO MANALI  (255 KM / 7 - 8 Hrs ) (BREAKFAST & DINNER)
   After breakfast Proceed for Manali (7 - 8 hrs) Enroute Visit enchanting Kullu Valley on River Beas & Kulu Shawl Factori. Arrive manali at late Evening. transfer to Hotel. Overnight Manali.
Day 05 :    MANALI - SNOW POINT - SOLANG VALLEY - MANALI (80 KM RETURN) (BREAKFAST & DINNER)
   After breakfast visit Atal Tunul & Solang Valley. Here you can enjoy ski Himalayan cable car rides, adventure activities like Paragliding, Zorbing, Ice Ski etc. (on your cost On). Evening Free time for Shopping on Mall road. Overnight Manali.
Day 06 :    MANALI (BREAKFAST & DINNER)
   After B/F local sight seen like Vashishta Bath- The hot water sulphur spring, ancient Hedamba Temple, Back to Hotel, Lunch, Rest of day free for leisure. Overnight Manali.
Day 07 :    MANALI TO CHANDIGARH  ( 280 KM) (BREAKFAST & DINNER)
   After breakfast Proceed for Chandigarh. Arrive late Evening, Transfar to Hotel. Overnight Chandigarh.
Day 08 :    DROP TO CHANDIGARH / AMBALA RAILWAY STATION / AIRPORT (ONLY BREAKFAST )
   After breakfast Visit Rock Garden , Sukh Sagar Lake. Later Drop to Chandigarh / Ambala Railway Station / Airport, to board the Train / Flight. Tour End With Memoreble mamories With  Shree Kanaiya Travels.
