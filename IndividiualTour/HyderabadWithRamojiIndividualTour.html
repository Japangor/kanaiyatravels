Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    ARRIVE HYDERABAD
   Arrive Hyderabad station / airport and reach our hotel directly. On arrival checkin at hotel & freshen up. Day will be free at leisure to relax. Night halt at Hyderabad.
Day 02 :    HYDERABAD LOCAL SIGHTSEEN
   After breakfast, proceed to Golconda Fort, Charminar, Salar Jung Museum, Birla temple, After lunch visit to Snow world , Hussain Sagar Lake, NTR gardens, which is one of the best garden in HyderaBed City later enjoy the Laser show (at Your Own cost), which shows culture of HyderaBed. Night halt at HyderaBad.
Day 03 :    HYDERABAD - RAMOJI - HYDERABAD
   After breakfast proceed to explore Ramoji Film city. one of the biggest film city of Asia. Lunch will be in Ramoji. Evening free for shopping & return back to hotel. Night halt at Hyderabad.
Day 04 :    DROP TO HYDERABAD RAILWAY STATION
   After breakfast check/out time from hotel 12:00 noon & day will be free at your leisure. Transfer to Hyderabad Station to proceed for home destination with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
