Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    DELHI / CHANDIGARH TO MANALI
   Pick up From Delhi Railway Station / Airport at Evening & Proceed for Manali (15 - 16 Hrs) Or Pick up From Chandigarh Railway Station / Airport at Evening and Proceed for Manali (10 Hrs). Overnight Journey.
Day 02 :    MANALI
   Morning arrive at Manali, Full day free for Rest and Shopping. Overnight Manali. 
Day 03 :    MANALI TO SARCHU
   Early morning Leave for Sarchu cross through Rohtang Pass (13050 ft) enter to Lahaul Valley,visit Raja Gyepan temple at Sissu Village, Also crossing  Baralacha Pass (16020 ft) a beautiful drive on high altitude to be remembered for life time. Overnight Sarchu.
Day 04 :    SARCHU TO LEH
   Morning leave for Leh, through Nakeela and Lachangla Pass 16,617 ft., Skyangchu Thang (Plateau on Stretch of 42 Kms), Tanglang La Pass 17,585ft, 2nd highest motorable road in the world, reach upshi where your drive along with Indus river. Overnight Leh.
Day 05 :    LEH LOCAL (TOTAL 60 KM)
   Full day sightseeing of Shey Palace, Thicksey, Sindhu Darshan & Rancho School. After lunch visit Shanti Stupa, Military Museum (Hall Of Frame). Overnight Leh.
Day 06 :    LEH - NUBRA VALLEY (120 KM )
   After breakfast Drive to Nubra valley (4 -5 Hrs), via Khardungla (Highest motorable road in the world, 18,380 ft) reach deskit by afternoon, rest of the day free for exploring Deskit & Hundur villages, two hump camel safari in sand dunes or one can have a pleasant soft trek between Hundur to Deskit village. Overnight Nubra Valley. (Tent)
Day 07 :    NUBRA VALLEY - PANGONG LAKE (140 KM)
   After breakfast proceed for Pangong Lake (4-5 Hrs) at 14,500 ft hight & Length 130 Kms & Width 5.6 Kms Shared by Two Countries India & China, a Highest Salt Water Lake in the World, Enjoy the beauty of lake on Banks of the Lake while appreciating the changing Colors of fascinating high Altitude Lake. Overnight Pangong Lake (Tent).
Day 08 :    PANGONG LAKE - LEH (160 KM)
   After Breakfast leave by 8.00 am for Leh (5 Hrs) by crossing Changla Pass 17,350 ft. Third Highest Motorable road in the world. Arrive at noon. Evening Free time for visit Leh Bazar. Overnight Leh .
Day 09 :    LEH - ALCHI - LAMAYARU - MULBAK - KARGIL (230 KM)
   Drive to Kargil Enroute pass Namika-la (3,697 Mts.), Mulbekh where there is a Chamba statue carved out of a single rock approximately 50 ft. high, Fatu-la (4,087 Mts.) which is the highest pass on Leh - Srinagar road and visit Lamayuru monastery, After viewing fascinating view of granite colour soil rocks, drive down through kangaroo curves to Kargil. Overnight at Kargil.
Day 10 :    KARGIL -DRASS - SONMARG - SRINAGAR (210 KM)
   Early Morning 4.00am Proceed to Srinagar via Drass (Second Coldest inhabited place in World), visit Kargil war museum, Minimarg, Zoji la (3033 Mts.) and Sonmarg . After crossing the Jimmy Gate on Zoji la we enter Kashmir Valley - a lush green region that is exactly in contrast to the barren lands of Ladakh. Arrive at Srinagar in the evening. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 11 :    DROP TO UDHAMPUR RAILWAY STATION (230 KM) OR DROP TO JAMMU RAILWAY STATION (295 KM)
   After breakfast we proceed to Udhampur Railway Station (8-9 Hrs) OR Jammu station (12 -13 Hrs. journey) to board Your Train. with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passengers travelling by air has to reach Srinagar airport (1 Hrs) at their own expense. (Tour END)
