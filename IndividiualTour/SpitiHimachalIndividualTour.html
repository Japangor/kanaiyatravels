Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    CHANDIGARH – SHIMLA (115 KM)
   Pick Up From Ambala / Chandigarh railway station OR Chandigarh Airport and proceed to Shimla (6 - 7 Hrs). Enroute visit Rock Garden & Pinjore Garden In Chandigarh. Arrive Shimla at late evening transfer to Hotel.. Overnihgt Shimla.
Day 02 :    SHIMLA - SARAHAN - SANGLA  (240 KM)
   After breakfast proceed for Sangla (8-9 hrs). Arrive Sangla & Check in to the hotel. O/n Sangla.
Sangla : The name Sangla itself is engulfed in romantic lore. Sang is said to mean 'light' and la of course means 'pass' in the Tibetan language. In other words it is the 'pass of light'. Sangla village is surrounded with gigantic Kinner-Kailash peak (6,050m) towering over it.
Day 03 :    SANGLA - CHITKUL - SANGLA  (25 KM)
    (25 kms one way) : After breakfast proceed for Chitkul. The drive is one of the most fascinating, pine trees slowly giving way to ‘Bhojpatra’/Birch trees and then the tree line disappears and one reaches the meadowland. Nagasthi is the last border outpost and short walk from Chitkul. Civilians are not allowed beyond this point. The walk is a level one and slightly going down the path through a pastureland and fields overlooking the freely flowing Baspa. A 1.5 hrs walk takes you to the outpost where the Jawans’ smiling faces greet you. Back to Sangla. O/n Sangla.
Day 04 :    SANGLA - KALPA  (50 KM)
   After breakfast proceed for Kalpa (2670 Mtrs - 49 Kms – 4/5 hrs). Kalpa is District Headquarter of Kinnaur. Arrive Kalpa & Check into the hotel. Each & every corner of Kalpa facing Jorkanden & Kinner Kailash Peaks, considered as one of the mythical homes of Lord Shiva & by its side is a 79 foot rock formation that resembles a Shivlinga that changes the colour as the day passes & is visible to the naked eye on a clear day. O/n Kalpa.
Day 05 :    KALPA - TABO  (160 KM)
   After breakfast proceed for Tabo (3050 mts – 6/7 hrs). Check into the hotel. Later visit Tabo Monastery. O/n Tabo.
Day 06 :    TABO - KAZA (50 KM)
   After breakfast proceed for Kaza (3600 mts-5/6 hrs). En route visit Dhankar Monastery. The headquarters of Spiti sub-district, Kaza is situated on the bank of river Spiti. O/n Kaza.
Day 07 :    KAZA
   Today visit Ki Monastery (13,500 ft), the largest monastery in Spiti Valley established in 11th century. It has ancient Buddhist scrolls and paintings and also houses the largest number of Buddhist monks and nuns. Later visit KIBBER (13800 ft – 11 km) beyond Ki Monastery, known for being the highest inhabited and motorable villages in the world). Visit Comic village. O/n Kaza.
Day 08 :    KAZA - CHANDRA TAL LAKE - MANALI (210 KM)
   After breakfast proceed for Manali (10-11rs) via Chandra Tal Lake , Kunzum pass & Rohtang Pass. Reach Manali by evening and check into the hotel. O/n Manali.
Day 09 :    MANALI
   Full day free for personal activities. O/n Manali.
Day 10 :    MANALI - CHANDIGARH (310 KM)
   After breakfast proceed for Chandigarh (9-10 hrs). Reach Chandigarh by evening. Check into the hotel. O/n Chandigarh.
Day 11 :    DROP TO CHANDIGARH AIRPORT / RAILWAY STATION
   Today morning drop at Chandigarh airport/ Railway station for your final departure. The tour ends here with the sweet memories of Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
