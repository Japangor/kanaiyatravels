Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    ARRIVE KATRA (SHREE MATA VAISHNODEVI)
   Tour participants are requested to report Kanaiya Travels ’ Manager, on arrival at Katra Hotel . After Lunch can intrested are start for Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra by Horse(Rs. 1800/- ), Dolly (Rs. 4000/-) OR Walk(Free) (Return 28 km), (Also avaliable Helicopter Sarvice for Darshan (Only Day Time), Book Your Helicopter Ticket Directly from https://www.maavaishnodevi.org ). Overnight Katra.
Day 02 :    KATRA - PAHELGAM (230 KM)
   Morning After Breakfast Proceed for Pahelgam (8 to 9 Hrs), Enroute pass asia largest Tunel at Nasri, Pidah, Banihal, Anantnag.. Arrive Pahelgam at Late Evening. Transfer to Hotel. Overnight Pahelgam. 
Day 03 :    PAHELGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley. After Lunch Evening Visit Kashmir Vally ,Dabyan Valley, Baisaran Vally, By horse riding ( please be aware, here you can be cheated by horse riders) Evening Free time for Photographi at Lidder River, opposite Our Hotels. Overnight Pahelgam.
Day 04 :    PAHELGAM - GULMARG (140 KM )
   After breakfast proceed to Gulmarg (4-5 hrs. journey ). It is situated at 8825 feet above Sea level..Enroute visit Surya Temple at Muturn, Visit Cricket Bat Factory at Avantipura, Safron Firm at Pampore, Arrive Gulmarg Transfer to Hotel. After lunch Enjoy Horse Riding, Visit Shiv Temple ,Rest Of the day is free for Shopping & rest .Overnight Gulmarg. (please be aware, here you can be cheated by horse riders)
Day 05 :    GULMARG
   In the morning a walk upto Gandola Cable Car Station (1 To 2 Km) to enjoy Gandola Cable Car Ride (at Your Own Cost) & enjoy snow at this beautiful snow point. rest of the day is free time for enjoy photography, horse riding. ( please be aware, here you can be cheated by horse riders). Overnight Gulmarg. 
Day 06 :    GULMARG - SRINAGAR (60 KM)
   After breakfast Check out From Hotel & walk upto Gandola Cable Car Station(1.5 Km) to enjoy Gandola Cable Car Ride (At Your Own Cost) Enjoy snow at this beautiful snow point. Afternoon proceed to Srinagar (2 hrs journey). Enroute Visit & Shooping at Apple Firm and Showl & Dress Factory. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 07 :    SRINAGAR LOCAL
   Early morning visit Sri Shankaracharya Temple.(1100 ft. above surface level of the main city on the hill) Climb 270 steps to reach the temple from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Srinagar. After breakfast visit Tulip Garden (If Open), Moghul Gardens i.e. Chashmeshahi - The Royal Spring, Nishat Baug - Garden of Bliss and Shalimar - Garden of Love. Enjoy Shikara Ride . (At Your Own Coast). covering Nehru Park, Charchinar and Floating Market. . Overnight Srinagar.
Day 08 :    SRINAGAR - SONMARG - SRINAGAR (170 KM RETURN)
   Today`s full day trip to Sonmarg, which is located at 85 kms from Srinagar Sonmarg means meadow of gold. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point (at your own cost). Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass (Subject to Military Permission) Or Thajiwas By Local Texi .(at your own cost), Evening return back to Srinagar. Enroute Enjoy River Rafting & visit Khir Bhavani. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 09 :    DROP TO UDHAMPUR RAILWAY STATION (230 KM) OR DROP TO JAMMU RAILWAY STATION (295 KM)
   After breakfast we proceed to Udhampur Railway Station (8-9 Hrs) OR Jammu station (10 -11 Hrs. journey) to board Your Train. with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passengers travelling by air has Drop to Srinagar airport. Tour END
