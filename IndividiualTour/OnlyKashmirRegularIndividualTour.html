Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    JAMMU TO PAHELGAM (290 KM) OR SRINAGAR TO PAHELGAM (90 KM)
   Pick Up from Jammu / Udhampur Railway Station and Proceed for Pahelgam (9 - 10 Hrs) Enroute pass asia largest Tunel at Nasri, Pidah, Banihal, Anantnag. OR Pick Up from Srinagar Airport and Proceed for Pahelgam(3 Hrs). Arrive Pahelgam Transfer to Hotel. Overnight Pahelgam
Day 02 :    PAHELGAM
   Morning After Breakfast Visit Kashmir Vally ,Dabyan Valley, Baisaran Vally, By horse riding ( please be aware, here you can be cheated by horse riders) at this beautiful place, Evening is free time for shopping / rest.Overnight Pahalgam..Overnight Pahelgam.
Day 03 :    PAHELGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley. Evening Free time for Photographi at Lidder River, opposite Our Hotels. Overnight Pahelgam.
Day 04 :    PAHELGAM - SRINAGAR ( 90 KM )
   After breakfast proceed to Srinagar (3- hrs. journey ). It is situated at 8825 feet above Sea level..Enroute visit Surya Temple at Muturn, Visit Cricket Bat Factory at Avantipura, Safron Firm at Pampore, Arrive Srinagar Transfer to Hotel.. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 05 :    SRINAGAR - SONMARG - KHIR BHAWANI - SRINAGAR  ( 170 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast full day trip to Sonmarg (Subject to Military Permission), which is located at 90 kms from Srinagar at 2740 mtrs. above Sea level. Sonmarg means meadow of gold. Sonmarg is the home of many beautiful & serene lakes. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point (at your own cost). Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass (Subject to Military Permission) Or Thajiwas By Local Texi .(at your own cost) Evening return Srinagar. Enroute visit Khir Bhavani Temple. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 06 :    SRINAGAR LOCAL
   Early morning visit Sri Shankaracharya Temple.(1100 ft. above surface level of the main city on the hill) Climb 270 steps to reach the temple from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Srinagar. After breakfast visit Moghul Gardens i.e. Chashmeshahi - The Royal Spring, Nishat Baug - Garden of Bliss and Shalimar - Garden of Love. Enjoy Shikara Ride in Nageen Lake / Dal lake. (At Your Own Coast). covering Nehru Park, Charchinar and Floating Market. . Overnight Srinagar.
Day 07 :    SRINAGAR - GULMARG - SRINAGAR ( 130 KM RETURN )
   After breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg (2-3 Hrs)(9,500 ft. above sea level). En route pick up overcoats, gloves and boots, available on hire (optional), at Tangmarg. From Gulmarg take Gandola Ropeway (At Your Own Coast) for Khilanmarg (10,050ft. above the sea level). Have fun at the Snow Point.. Evening back Srinagar. Enroute visit & Shopping .Apple & Dryfruit Firm. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 08 :    DROP TO UDHAMPUR RAILWAY STATION (230 KM) OR DROP TO JAMMU RAILWAY STATION (295 KM)
   After breakfast we proceed to Udhampur / Jammu Railway Station (8-9 Hrs) to board Your Train. with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passengers travelling by air has Drop to Srinagar airport. Tour END
