Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    ARRIVE : JAIPUR
   Meet & Greet on arrival at Jaipur Airport / Railway Station & proceed to hotel, Jaipur popularly called as Pink city. On arrival check-into Hotel. Evening visit Birla Mandir & Chokhi-Dhani. Overnight stay at Jaipur.
Day 02 :    JAIPUR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING 
   After breakfast, Sightseeing tour of Jaipur which includes Amer Fort, Jantar Mantar, Maharaja Palace and Hawa Mahal. Evening free time for shopping. Night halt at Jaipur.
Day 03 :    JAIPUR TO JODHPUR (340 KM / 7 - 8 Hrs)
   After breakfast proceed to Jodhpur, On the way visit Ajmer Moinuddin Chishti Darga & Pushkar Bhramaji Temple. On Arrival, Jodhpur transfar to hotel. Night halt at Jodhpur.
Day 04 :    JODHPUR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING 
   After breakfast visit Jodhpur local sightseeing (Visit Mehrangarh Fort, Umed Bhawan, Mandor Garden). Overnight Stay at Jodhpur
Day 05 :    JODHPUR TO JAISALMER (280 KM / 6 - 7 Hrs)
   Early & After breakfast proceed to Jaisalmer, On the way visit Ram devra (Ramdev peer) Temple. Arrive at Evening and visit Jaisalmer local sightseeing (Visit Patavo Ki Haweli, Sonar Fort, Nathmal Ki Haweli etc.) Overnight stay at Jaisalmer.
Day 06 :    JAISALMER - SAND DUNES
  

Morning free time for visit Jaisalmer local sightseeing. After lunch proceed to Sam Sand Dunes. On arrival, enjoy Camel Safari at dessert, Rajasthani Folk Dance, Camp Fire and Dinner. Overnight stay at Swiss Tent at Jaisalmer

Day 07 :    JAISALMER TO JODHPUR : DROP (310 KM / 8 Hrs)
   After breakfast proceed to Jodhpur station/airport or Jaisalmer airport to board your flight. Tour End With Memoreble mamories With Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
