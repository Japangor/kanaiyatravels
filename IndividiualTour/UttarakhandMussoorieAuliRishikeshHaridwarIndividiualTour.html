Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    DELHI TO MUSSOORIE(280 KM / 8 -9 Hrs) OR HARIDWAR / DEHRADUN TO MUSSOORIE
   Pick Up From New Delhi Railway Station / Airport at Morning and proceed to Mussoorie (9-10 hrs. journey) OR Pick Up From Haridwar Railway Station / Dehradun Airport and proceed to Mussoorie (1 -2 hrs. journey). Evening visit Mall Road for shopping. Night halt at Mussoorie.
Day 02 :    MUSSOORIE SIGHTSEEING
   Wake up early in the morning and enjoy a nature walk at the mall road. Have your hot and delicious breakfast at the hotel. Post breakfast proceeds to explore Kempty falls, Devbhumi wax museum, Mussoorie lake, Camels back road, Cloud’s end, and Bhatta falls. Lunch during the sightseeing. Evening return back to the hotel. Evening explores Gun Hills by ropeway on your own from the Mall Road. Dinner and overnight stay at the Mussoorie.
Day 03 :    MUSSOORIE TO CHOPTA (185 KM / 7 Hrs)
   Wake up, Get refreshed and refuel yourself with a heavy breakfast. Check out at 08.30 AM from the hotel and proceeds to Rudraprayag via covering Surkanda Devi temple, Eco Park, Dhanaulti, Kanatal, Chamba, New Tehri, Srinagar, and Rudraprayag on the way. Evening arrives, get check-in into the Hotel, dinner and overnight stay at Rudraprayag.
Day 04 :    RUDRAPRAYAG - CHOPTA - JOSHIMATH (180 KM / 7 HR)
   After Breakfast, Proceed to Chopta (75 kms), Chopta is popularly known as ‘Mini Switzerland’ in India. Chopta, is an amazing natural beauty destination lying in the laps of Uttarakhand Himalayas offers scenic views of the imposing Himalayan range including Trishul, Nanda Devi and Chaukhamba. Proceed from Chopta to Joshimath (100 Kms drive). Night Stay at Joshimath.
Day 05 :    JOSHIMATH - AULI - JOSHIMATH (32 Km Return)
   Proceed from Joshimath to Auki by Cable Car or Road drive. Auli is famous for its 3 KM long skiing slope. Auli is located at a distance of 16 KM from Joshimath by Road. There are roads connecting Joshimath to Auli but best way to travel to Auli is by cable car. Auli is located at a height of 2750 meter is famous for Ski, many national and international competitions on winter sports like Ski are organized during Jan to March months when Auli gets covered by more than 6 feet of snow. Evening back Joshimath. Overnight Joshimath.
Day 06 :    JOSHIMATH - RISHIKESH (255 KM/8 HR)
   After Breakfast Check-out from the hotel at 08.30 AM and proceed directly to Rishikesh. Explore Nandprayag, Karnprayag, Rudraprayag, Srinagar, Devprayag, and Byasi on the way. Evening arrives, get check-in into the hotel. Explore the surroundings and get blessed by participating in the Ganga Aarti at Rishikesh. Back to the hotel, having dinner and overnight stay at Rishikesh.
Day 07 :    ADVENTURES OF RISHIKESH TO HARIDWAR ( 30 KM / 1 Hrs)
   Morning to Afternoon Sightseeing which includes Laxmanjhula Or Ramjhulla. Parmarthdham, & Geeta Bhavan. OR guest can enjoy adventurous activities like Rafting, Zip liner, Bungee Jumping, Flying Fox etc. (activities own cost), Afternoon proceed to Haridwar (1.00 Hrs). On arrival, visit (By Auto Riksha at your Own Cost) Chandi Devi, Mansa Devi , Bharat Mata temple, Daksh Mandir, Vaishnodevi Mandir. Evening we attend the famous Ganga Aarti on Har ki Pauri Ghat, Transfar to Hotel. Night halt at Haridwar.
Day 08 :    HARIDWAR - DELHI (200 KM/6 HR)
   Morning to Afternoon free time for visit Haridwar Sightseeing Afternoon Transfar to Haridwar Railway Station / Dehradun Airport OR Delhi Station / Airport.
