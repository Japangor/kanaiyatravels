Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI - AMBALA / CHANDIGARH
   Depart For Ambala By 12471 Swaraj Exp. OR Chandigarh by 122451 Chandigarh Super Fast Train From Bandra Terminal. Overnight Train Journey.
Day 02 :    ARRIVE AMBALA / CHANDIGARH
   Arrive Ambala railway station at 8.00 am as per 12471 Swaraj Exp. Or Arrive Chandigarh at Evening 16.00pm By 22451 Chandigarh Super Fast.and proceed to Hotel. Enroute visit Rock Garden & Pinjore Garden Overnihgt Chandigarh.
Day 02 :    CHANDIGARH - KUFRI - SHIMLA (155 KM)
   Early & After Breakfast Drive to Kufri (5-6 Hrs) , visit Kufri By Horse, enjoy horse riding (please be aware, here you can be cheated by horse riders). Back Shimla at noon. Evening we will have free time for shopping at Mall Road. Overnight Shimla.
Day 03 :    SHIMLA - KULU - MANALI  (280 KM)
   After B/F start for Manali (App. 280 Kms. /12 Hrs.) situated on 5500 feet. Height, Lunch on the way, Enroute visit Kullu Shawl Factory, Arrive Manali at Late Evening, Overnight Manali.
Day 04 :    MANALI - ROHTANG PASS - SOLANGVALLEY - MANALI (110 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast full day visit at snow point of Manali i.e Rohtang / Madhi / Gulaba (Till our vehicle can reach) , Rohtang Pass is situated at 13,500 feet above sea level OR visit Solang Vallley here you can enjoy Ski Himalayan Cable Car Ride , adventure activities like Paragliding, Zorbing , Ice Ski etc. Evening free for leisure or shopping at Mall road. Overnight Manali.
Day 05 :    MANALI
   After B/F local sight seen like Vashishta Bath- The hot water sulphur spring, ancient Hedamba Temple, Back to Hotel, Lunch, Rest of the day free for leisure, Overnight Manali.
Day 06 :    MANALI - KULU - DHARMSALA / CHAMUNDAJI ( 210 KM)
   Early and after breakfast proceed for Dharamsala, (9 - 10Hrs), Enroute enjoy River Rafting (Self Expense), and Also Visit Kulu Shawl Factori . After arrive visit Dalai Lama Temple, And Chamundaji Temple. Overnight Chamundaji.
Day 07 :    CHAMUNDAJI - DALHOUSIE ( 170 KM)
   After B/F start for Dalhousie (App. 170 Kms. / 9 Hrs.) situated on 5500 ft. height, Lunch on the way, Arrive Dalhousie, Check into the Hotel, Overnight Dalhousie.
Day 08 :    DALHOUSIE - KHAJJIAR - DALHOUSIE (44 KM RETURN)
   After B/F start for Khajjiar popularity called the Mini Switzerland where you can enjoy the Horse Riding, Back to the Hotel, Lunch, Rest of the day free for leisure, Overnight Dalhousie.
Day 09 :    DALHOUSIE - GOLDEN TEMPLE - WAGAH BORDER - AMRUTSAR  ( 180 KM)
   Early Morning (6.00AM) after breakfast leave for Amrutsar. (6-7 Hrs). Lunch Enroute, Arrive Amrutsar & visit Golden Temple , Histroricle Jaliawala Bagh and Evening Enjoy Ind - Pak Militry Pared on Wagha Border. Overnight Amritsar.
Day 10 :    AMRITSAR - KATRA (VAISHNODEVI) ( 255 KM)
   Early Morning (6.00AM) after breakfast leave for Katra. (7 Hrs). Arrive Katra & Transfer to Hotel , After Lunch can intrested are start for Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra Return back at Late Night (Return 28 km), ( Horse - Rs. 1800/- , Dolly Rs. 3500/- Also avaliable Helicopter Sarvice for Darshan Rs. 2400/- Return (Only Day Time), Helicopter droped 3 km before Temple, Book Your Helicopter Ticket Directly from https://www.maavaishnodevi.org ). Overnight Katra.
Day 11 :    KATRA - MUMBAI
   Morning After Breakfast Drop to Katra Railway Station to Board Your Train for Mumbai 12472 Swaraj Exp. Overnight Train. OR Drop to Jammu Airport For Fly to Mumbai.
Day 12 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai at Evening. Tour End With Memoreble mamories With Shree Kanaiya Travels.
