Days Tour Detail
28 Feb :    ARRIVE AMRITSAR
   Pickup at 7.00 p.m. (as per the schedule of 12925 PASCHIM EXPRESS) & transfer to hotel. Overnight Amritsar.
29 Feb :    AMRITSAR
   After breakfast sightseeing Tour of Jalianwala Baug, Golden Temple . After lunch visit the Wagha Border famous for military parade (Flag march) by India and Pakistan’s Army..Overnihgt Amritsar.
01 March :    AMRITSAR - DALHOUSIE  (180 KM)
   After breakfast proceed to Dalhousie (7-8 hrs. journey), reach Dalhousie and the day is free for rest. Overnight Dalhousie.
02 March :    DALHOUSIE - KHAJJIAR - DALHOUSIE  (44 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast we visit Khajjiar for a breath taking view of the ground covered with pine trees. After lunch,enjoy shopping at the famous Tibetan Market .Overnight Dalhousie.
03 March :    DALHOUSIE - KANGRA - DHARAMSHALA  (170 KM )
   After breakfast we will proceed to Dharamshala (7-8 hrs.Journey ), en route we will visit Kangra Temple, After Arrive Visit Lama Temple, Bhagsunath Temple , Etc.  Overnight Dharamshala.
04 March :    DHARAMSHALA - CHAMUNDAJI (20 Km)
   After Breakfast Proceed for Chamundaji (1 Hrs). Enroute Visit Dharamsala Cricket Stadiam. Arrive Chamundaji visit Chamundaji Temple. Overnight Chamundaji.
05 March :    CHAMUNDAJI - JWALADEVI   PINJORE (CHANDIGARH) ( 250 KM)
   After breakfast full day travel to Chandigarh (8-10 Hrs), Enroute Visit Jwalaji Temple. Arrive Chandigarh , Visit Pinjore Garden. (If Time is Spare Also Visit Rock & Rose Garden in Chandigarh.) Overnight Pinjore (Chandigarh).
06 March :    DROP TO CHANDIGARH RAILWAY STATION (20 KM)
   After breakfast, Drop to Chandigarh Railway Station (1 Hrs). Tour End With Memoreble Memories With Kanaiya Travels.
