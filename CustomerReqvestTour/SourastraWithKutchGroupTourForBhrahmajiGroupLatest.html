Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI - BHUJ
   Depart for Bhuj By 22955 Kutch Exp. Train From Bandra Terminals at Evening 17.10pm. Overnihgt Train Journey.
Day 02 :    ARRIVE BHUJ
   Arrive Bhuj at Morning, Transfer to Hotels. After lunch visit places like Aina Mahal Museum, Pragmahal Museum, Bhartiya Sanskriti Darsan. Overnihgt Bhuj.
Day 03 :    BHUJ - DHORDO VILLAGE - KALO DUNGAR - BHUJ (220 KM RETURN)
   Early Morning After Breakfast Today, get ready to explore the Banni and Pachcham regions and the edge of the Great Rann of Kutch. Visit Dhordo village & Khavda village. Later visit Kalo Dungar (Black Hill). Return Bhuj at late Evening. Overnihgt Bhuj.
Day 04 :    BHUJ - MATA NO MUDH - NARAYAN SAROVER - MANDVI (315 KM)
   After & Early After breakfast proceeds to Mandvi, Enroute visit Mata No Madha, Narayan Sarover, After visit Drive to Mandvi. Arrive at late night - transfer to hotels. Overnihgt Mandvi.
Day 05 :    MANDVI - DWARKA (445 KM)
   After Breakfast Check out from Hotel Visit Vijay Villas Palace – Summer retreat of the royal family. After visit Drive to Dwarka, Enroute visit Jamnagar City Tour, Arrive Dwarka at Late Evening. Overnight Dwarka.
Day 06 :    DWARKA - BAT DWARKA - NAGESHWER - DWARKA
   After Breakfast Drive to Okha Visit Bat Dwarka By Boat (at Your Own Coast). Jyotiling Nageshwer Temple, & Rukmani Temple. After Lunch Evening Free time for Visit Dwarkesh Temple and Enjoy at Beach. Overnight Dwarka.
Day 07 :    DWARKA -- SOMNATH (TOTAL 300 KM)
   After Breakfast proceed for Somnath, Enroute Visit Harsiddhi Mata Temple, & Porbunder Local Sight Seen. and Reach Somnath. Rest Of Time Free For Darshan for Somnath Mahadev. & Triveni Sangam. Overnight Somnath.
Day 08 :    SOMNATH - DIU - SASAN GIR (160 KM)
   After Breakfast Visit Bhalka Tirth. After Visit Proceed for Diu. After Arrive visit Diu Fort, Museum,and Enjoy at Beach. After visit Diu, Drive to Sasan. Overnight Sasan.
Day 09 :    SASAN GIR - JUNAGARH (60 KM)
   After And Early breakfast 4.30 am Take Jungle Safari By Jeep/Bus (at Your Own Cost). After Safari Proceed for Junagarh. After Arrive visit Junagarh Fort and Zoo. Overnight Junagarh.
Day 10 :    JUNAGARH - VIRPUR - GONDAL - CHOTILA - SARANGPUR (250 KM)
   After Breakfast Proceed for Sarangpur, Enroute visit and Darshan at Virpur Jalaram Temple, Gondal Bhubneshwari Mata Temple, Chamundaji at Chotila and then Drive to Sarangpur. Overnight Sarangpur.
Day 11 :    SARANGPUR - AHMEDABAD (150 KM) - MUMBAI
   Morning Free time for Darshan For Sarangpur Hanumanji Temple, & Swami Narayan Temple, After Breakfast drive Ahmedabad to Board Your Train for Mumbai. Overnight Train.
Day 12 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai at morning. Tour End With Memoreble mamories With Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
