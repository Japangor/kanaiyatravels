Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    ARRIVE KATRA (SHREE MATA VAISHNODEVI)
   Tour participants are requested to report Kanaiya Travels ' Manager, on arrival at Katra Hotel . After Lunch can intrested are start for Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra by Horse (Rs. 1800/- ), Dolly (Rs. 4000/-) OR Walk(Free) (Return 28 km), Also avaliable Helicopter Sarvice for Darshan (Only Day Time), The helicopter will drop you 3 kilometers before the temple, Book Your Helicopter Ticket Directly from https://www.maavaishnodevi.org ). Overnight Katra.
Day 02 :    KATRA (Full Day Vaishnodevi Darshan)
   Full Day For Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan..Evening Free time for Shopping. Overnight Katra. 
Day 03 :    KATRA TO PAHELGAM (240 KM)
   After breakfast, proceed to Pahalgam On the way passes Nasri Tunnel (Asia's largest tunnel), Pidah (famous for Rajma-rice and Pomegranate Dana Chutney), Banihal Tunnel and Anantnag Reach Pahalgam late evening.. Night stay in Pahalgam.
Day 04 :    PAHELGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley (Include In Cost) the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley. After Lunch Visit Aru Valley by Unian Vehicle (Your Own cost). Evening free time for Photographi on Ledder River. Overnight at Pahelgam.
Day 05 :    PAHELGAM
   After breakfast, take horse ride to Kashmir Valley, Dabyan Valley, Baisern Valley and Mini Switzerland (at your own cost). Enjoy photography at this beautiful place. There is free time in the evening to visit the market and do shopping. Overnight stay Pahalgam.
Day 06 :    PAHALGAM - GULMARG (140 KM)
   In the morning after breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg (4 – 5 hrs) En route visit & shoping at Apple Garden at Pahalgam Road, Sun Temple at Matern, Cricket Bat Factory at Awantipura and Saffron Firm at Pampore. On the way you can rent overcoats, gloves and shoes from Tangmarg (optional). Reach Gulmarg and transfer to hotels. Overnight stay Gulmarg.
Day 07 :    GULMARG
   After Breakfast, walk upto Gandola Cable Car Station (1.5 Km from Hotels) to enjoy Gandola Cable Ride Enjoy snow at this beautiful snow point. After lunch Enjoy Horse Riding. Overnight Gulmarg
Day 08 :    GULMARG - SRINAGAR (65 KM) & SRINAGAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING
   After Breakfast proceed for Srinagar (2 Hrs), Enroute Visit Apple Farm & Dry Fruit Farm and Showl & Dress Factory, Arrive Srinagar Transfer to Hotel / Houseboat. After lunch visit Moghul Gardens i.e. Chashmeshahi - Nishat Baug, Shalimar & Tulip ( If Open). Evening Enjoy Shikara Ride by covering Meena bazar and Floating Market. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 09 :    SRINAGAR - SONMARG - SRINAGAR (170 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast full day trip to Sonmarg, which is located at 85 kms from Srinagar Sonmarg means meadow of gold Sonmarg is the home of many beautiful & serene lakes. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point. Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass (Subject to Military Permission) OR Thajiwas By Local Texi .(at your own cost), Evening return back to Srinagar. Enroute Enjoy River Rafting & visit Khir Bhavani Temple. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 10 :    SRINAGAR TO DUDHPATHRI OR YUSHMARG TO SRINAGAR (OPTIONAL & AT YOUR OWN RISK - HORSE RIDING POINT -- ONLY IF THE SITUATION IN KASHMIR IS 100% SAFE & GOOD )
   After breakfast, visit Shankaracharya Temple By Auto Riksha (1100 ft. above surface level of the main city on the hill, Climb 270 steps to reach the temple), from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Srinagar. After Lunch, we will drive to Dudhpathri OR Yushmarg (100 km return & horse riding point). Upon arrival take a pony on rent to visit other places & enjoy photography. (only if the condition of Kashmir is 100% good and safe we will travel to Yushmarg OR Dudhpathri). Evening Back Srinagar & free time for shopping. Nightstay at Srinagar.
Day 11 :    DROP TO UDHAMPUR  (180 KM) OR JAMMU (245 KM) RAILWAY STATION  OR DROP TO JAMMU RAILWAY STATION (245 KM) OR SRINAGAR AIRPORT
   Early & After breakfast we proceed to Udhampur Railway Station (6 Hrs) OR Jammu station
(8 Hrs) to board Your Train. with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passengers travelling by air has Drop to Srinagar airport. TOUR END
