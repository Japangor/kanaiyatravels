Days Tour Detail
14 April :    MUMBAI - DELHI
   Depart For Delhi By 22209 Mumbai - New Delhi Duronto Exp. From Mumbai Central at 23.00pm. Overnight Train Journey.
15 April :    DELHI - JAMMU
   Arrive New Delhi at Evening 16.30pm & Proceed for Jammu By 12425 JAMMU RAJDHANI Exp.at 20.30pm. Overnight Train Journey.
16 April :    JAMMU - KATRA (45 KM)
   Arrive Jammu at Morning and . Proceed to Katra (2 Hrs). After arrive Transfer to Hotel (Min. 2 -3 Hrs Wait For Room Alloted- Check In Time is 12.00). After Lunch can intrested are start for Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra by Horse (Rs. 2000/- ), Dolly (Rs. 3500 To 4000/-) OR Walk(Free) (Return 28 km), (Also avaliable Helicopter Sarvice for Darshan (Only Day Time), Helicopter droped 3 km before Temple, Book Your Helicopter Ticket Directly from www.maavaishnodevi.org . Overnight Katra.
17 April :    KATRA (SHREE MATA VAISHNODEVI)
   Full Day Free For Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan. Overnight Katra.
18 April :    KATRA - PAHALGAM  (270 KM)
   Early Morning After Breakfast Proceed for Pahalgam (10 to 11 Hrs), Enroute visit & lunch at Patnitop (If Road Is Open), Enroute pass asia largest Tunel at Banihal, Arrive Pahalgam at Late Evening. Transfer to Hotel. Overnight Pahalgam. 
19 April :    PAHALGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley (Include in Tour cost) the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley. Enjoy horse riding at this beautiful place, Evening is free for shopping Overnight Pahalgam..Overnight Pahelgam.
20 April :    PAHELGAM - AVANTIPORE - SAFRON FIRM - SRINAGAR (90 KM )
   After breakfast proceed for Srinagar. (3-4 Hrs), Enroute visit Safron Firm, Cricket Bat Factory, Avantipora Museam, Arrive Srinagar late afternoon. & transfer to Houseboat. After lunch day is free time for rest / Photographi. Overnight Srinagar.
21 April :   SRINAGAR - SONMARG - KHIR BHAWANI - SRINAGAR  ( 170 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast Chek out From Houseboat & full day trip to Sonmarg , which is located at 90 kms from Srinagar at 2740 mtrs. above Sea level. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point (at your own cost). Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass (Subject to Military Permission) Or Thajiwas By Local Texi .(at your own cost) Evening return back to Srinagar.Transfer to Hotel. Overnight Srinagar.
22 April :   SRINAGAR - GULMARG - SRINAGAR ( 130 KM RETURN )
   After breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg (2-3 Hrs)(9,500 ft. above sea level). En route pick up overcoats, gloves and boots, available on hire (optional), at Tangmarg. From Gulmarg take Gandola Ropeway (First Stage Rs. 800/- Include In Tour Cost) for Khilanmarg (10,500ft. above the sea level). Have fun at the Snow Point.. Evening back Srinagar. Enroute visit & Shopping .Apple & Dryfruit Firm. Overnight Srinagar.
23 April :   SRINAGAR LOCAL
   Early morning visit Sri Shankaracharya Temple. (Climb 280 steps to reach the temple ) from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Srinagar. After breakfast visit Moghul Gardens i.e. Chashmeshahi - The Royal Spring, Nishat Baug - Garden of Bliss and Shalimar - Garden of Love. Enjoy Shikara Ride in Dallake. Overnight Srinagar.
24 April :   SRINAGAR - JAMMU (295 KM)
   Early & After breakfast 5.00am, we proceed for Jammu (10 - 11 Hrs), Arrive at Late Evening, Transfer to Hotel. Rest of time for Shoping and Visit Raghunath Temple.. Overnight Jammu (Economi Hotel).
25 April :   JAMMU - MUMBAI
   After breakfast proceed for Mumbai By 12472 Swaraj Exp. at 11.00am from Jammu Railway Station. . Overnight Train Journey.
26 April :   ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai at evening 18.00 pm TourEND with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
