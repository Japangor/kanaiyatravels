Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI TO JABALPUR (3 AC Train Journey)
   Depart to Jabalpur by 12188 Garibrath Exp Train from Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminal at 13.30pm . Overnight train journey.
Day 02 :    ARRIVE JABALPUR & PROCEED TO KANHA ( 140 KM - 3 To 4 Hrs)
   Arrive at Jabalpur at morning & proceed to Kanha. After reaching Kanha complete check-in formalities at the resort / Hotels and freshen up yourself. Have lunch and get ready for the evening jeep safari in Kanha National Park (At Your Own Cost). In evening come back to the resort, enjoy tea/coffee. Dinner will be served on time, overnight stay at Kanha.
Day 03 :    KANHA TO JABALPUR ( 140 KM - 3 To 4 Hrs)
   After breakfast, Proceed to Jabalpur. arrive at Jabalpur rest of day Free time for relaxation. Overnight stay at Jabalpur.
Day 04 :    JABALPUR - PANCHMARHI
   After breakfast transfer to Panchmarhi. Enroute have lunch and  visit Beda Ghat (famous for marble rocks), Dhuwadhar Waterfalls. Late evening arrive at Panchmarhi & Transfar to Hotel. Overnight stay at Panchmarhi
Day 05 :    PANCHMARHI
   After breakfast proceed for Panchmarhi sightseeing.Visit Pandava Caves, Handi Khoh, Priyadarshani Valley, After lunch visit Jatashankar Temple, enjoy boating in Panchmarhi lake, & Evening enjoy Sunset point (Rajendra Giri - By Jeep - Own cost). Overnight stay at Panchmarhi.
Day 06 :    PANCHMARHI
   After breakfast, proceed to Bison Lodge, Bee Waterfalls and Rich Gadh. In the evening proceed to Dhup Gadh for Sunset (By Jeep - Your Own cost). Later free time for leisure. Overnight Panchmarhi.
Day 07 :    PANCHMARHI - UJJAIN ( 400 KM - 10 Hrs)
   After brakfast proceed to Ujjain. On arrival, visit to Mahakaleshwar temple( It is one of the famous Jyotirlinga Shrines of Shiva), Bada Ganesh, Harisiddhi mata temple , Wakankar Museum & Kaal Bhairav temple. Late evening transfar in Hotel. Overnight stay at Ujjain.
Day 08 :    UJJAIN
   Early Morning 3.00am Visit ( By Auto Riksha - Own Cost) Mahakaleshwara temple darshan With Bhasma Arty, After Breakfast visit Sandipani Ashram, Sidhavat Temple, Mangalnath Temple, Gadkalika Temple, Bharthari Gufa, Harrisiddhi Mata, Kaal Bhairav Temple. Overnight Ujjain.
Day 09 :    UJJAIN TO OMKARESHWER - MAMLESHWER TO INDORE ( 230 KM / Full Day Trip )
   After breakfast Drive to Omkaleshwer(4 Hrs). Omkareshwar  situated on  the banks of  Narmada was a sight for sore eyes. went in the Prime of the heat but the drive passing through a mini ghat was peacefull comfortable. Visit Omkaleshwar Jyotirling & Mamleshwar mahadev. Return to Indore. Overnight stay at Indore.
Day 10 :    INDORE TO MUMBAI ( 3 AC TRAIN JOURNEY )
   Morning free time for Rest. After Lunch check out from Hotel & visit Rajwada Palace, Lalbagh Palace, Zoo & Ganesh Mandire. Evening Drive to indore Railway Station & proceed for Mumbai By 12962 avantika Exp from indore Railway Station at evening 17.35pm. Overnight Train Journey.
Day 11 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai at Morning. Tour End With Memoreble mamories With Shree Kanaiya Travels.
