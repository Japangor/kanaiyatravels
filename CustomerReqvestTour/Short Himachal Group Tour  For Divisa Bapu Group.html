Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    LUDHIANA TO NAINA DEVI  (140 KM - 5 Hrs)
   Early Morning Proceed to Nainadevi (140 km / 4 -5 Hrs), Arrive at afternoon, and after lunch visit Nainadevi Temple (By Cable Car), Rest of Day free time for rest. . Nightstay at Nainadevi.
Day 02 :    NAINADEVI TO JWALAMUKHI TO CHAMUNDAJI  (180 KM - 5 - 6 Hrs Total)
   After breakfast, drive to Chamundaji (180 km - 6 -7 Hrs). Enroute visit Jwalamukhi Temple (1 km Trek). On Arrival at Chamundaji visit Chamundaji devi Temple. Late Evening transfar to hotel. Nightstay at Chamundaji.
Day 03 :    CHAMUNDAJI TO DHARAMSHALA TO KANGRA DEVI TO CHAMUNDAJI  (60 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast, drive to Kangra, On Arrival, visit Kangra devi Temple & can intrested are also visit Kangra Fort by Auto. Back to hotel for lunch. After Lunch, drive to Dharamshala. On Arrival visit Dalai Lama Temple & Bhakshunag Temple ( By Auto). After visit back to Hotel. Nightstay at Chamundaji.
Day 04 :    CHAMUNDAJI - DALHOUSIE ( 130 KM - 5 Hrs)
   After B/F start for Dalhousie (App. 130 Kms. / 5 Hrs.) situated on 5500 ft. height, Lunch on the way, Arrive Dalhousie, Check into the Hotel, Overnight Dalhousie.
Day 05 :    DALHOUSIE - KHAJJIAR - DALHOUSIE (44 KM RETURN)
   After B/F start for Khajjiar popularity called the Mini Switzerland where you can enjoy the Horse Riding (Self Expense), Back to the Hotel, Lunch, Rest of the day free for leisure, Overnight Dalhousie.
Day 06 :    DALHOUSIE - CHINTPURNI TO LUDHIANA ( 280 KM - 8 Hrs Total)
   After breakfast, leave for Ludhiana. (280 km / 8 Hrs). Enroute visit Shree Chintpurni Mata Temple. Arrive Ludhiana at Late Evening. Tour End With Memoreble mamories With Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
