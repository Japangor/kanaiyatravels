Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    DEPART FOR KATRA ( TRAIN JOURNEY)
   Departure for Katra By Train from Mumbai / Surat OR Departure for Katra by via Train from Ahmedabad - Jaipur - Indore - Delhi. Overnight Train Journey.
Day 02 :    ARRIVE KATRA (SHREE MATA VAISHNODEVI)
   Arrive at Katra and transfar in hotel. (Passengers traveling by flight will have to reach Katra Hotel at their own cost.) After dinner, interested travelers can start Shri Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra (28 kms return) by horse-dolly or on foot. Also helicopter service is available. Overnight stay in Katra. (Book your Helicopter ticket directly from website --- https://www.maavaishnodevi.org/ )
Day 03 :    KATRA (SHREE MATA VAISHNODEVI)
   Full Day Free For Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan. Overnight Katra.
Day 04 :    KATRA TO SRINAGAR (250 KM / 8 Hrs )
   After Breakfast Proceed for Srinagar (8 Hrs), . Enroute pass largest Tunel Nasri & Banihal. Arrive Srinagar and Transfer to Hotel. Evening free time for Enjoy Shikara Ride. Night at Srinagar. 
Day 05 :    SRINAGAR - SONMARG - SRINAGAR ( 170 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast full day trip to Sonmarg, which is located at 90 kms from Srinagar at 2740 mtrs. above Sea level. Sonmarg means meadow of gold Sonmarg is the home of many beautiful & serene lakes. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point. Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass OR Thajiwas (Subject to Military Permission & If Road is Open) By Local Texi. (at your own cost) . Evening return back to Srinagar. Nightstay at Srinagar.
Day 06 :    SRINAGAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING & SRINAGAR TO DUDHPATHARI OR YUSHMARG TO SRINAGAR
   Early and after breakfast morning 7.30, we will drive to Dudhpathri OR Yushmarg (100 km return & horse riding point). Upon arrival take a pony on rent to visit photography point. (only if the condition of Kashmir is 100% good and safe we will travel to Yushmarg or Dudhpathri - at customer's own risk). Afternon Back Srinagar & after Lunch, we will be visit Moghul Gardens (i.e. t Nishant Baug - Chashmeshahi Garden and Shalimar Garden). Nightstay at Srinagar. 
Day 07 :    SRINAGAR - GULMARG - SRINAGAR ( 130 KM RETURN )
   After breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg (3 Hrs - 9,500 ft. above sea level). Enroute pick up overcoats, gloves and boots, available on hire (optional) at Tangmarg. Arrive Gulmarg and walk upto Gandola Cable Car Station (1.2 Km from Parking) to enjoy Gandola Cable Ride, Enjoy Horse Riding & snow at this beautiful snow point. Evening back Srinagar. Overnight at Srinagar.
Day 08 :    SRINAGAR TO PAHELGAM (90 KM / 3 Hrs)
   Early morning visit Shankaracharya Temple.(1100 ft. above surface level of the main city on the hill) Climb 270 steps to reach the temple. After breakfast proceed to Pahalgam (90 km / 3 hrs) situated at an altitude of 7,200 ft above sea level on the banks of Lidder River, also known as the most beautiful hill station of India. On the way to Pahalgam you can see saffron, apple, walnut, cherry fields & Cricket Bat Factory in Awantipur & from their shops you can buy dry fruits, saffron & cricket bats.. Reach Pahalgam and transfer to hotel. In the evening enjoy photography of natural beauty in the heaven on earth. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.
Day 09 :    PAHALGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley (Include In Tour Cost) the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley. After lunch Visit Kashmir Vally, Dabyan Valley, Baisaran Vally (By horse at Your Own) riding at this beautiful place, Evening is free time for visit Market & shopping. Overnighthalt at Pahalgam.
Day 10 :    DROP TO UDHAMPUR / JAMMU RAILWAY STATION (10 Hrs)
   After breakfast we proceed to Udhampur Jammu Railway Station (8 - 9 hrs) from where we board the train to Mumbai / Surat / Vadodara via Delhi, Jaipur or Ahmedabad. Passengers who are traveling by flight will be reach Srinagar airport their own cost. Overnight train journey.
Day 11 :    ARRIVE DELHI - JAIPUR OR AHMEDABAD & PROCEED TO MUMBAI / SURAT BY NEXT TRAIN
   Arrive Delhi, Jaipur or Ahmedabad and proceed to Your home Destination by next train .
Day 12 :    ARRIVE AHMEDABAD / VADODARA / SURAT / MUMBAI
   Arrive at your destination as per the arrival time of train. TOUR END with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
