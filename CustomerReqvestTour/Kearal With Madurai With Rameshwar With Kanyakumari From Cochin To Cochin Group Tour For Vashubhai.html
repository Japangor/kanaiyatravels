Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :     MUMBAI / AHMEDABAD TO THRISSUR  ( By 3 AC Train ) ( Only Dinner From Train Pentri Car)
   Depart for Thrissur by Train from Ahmedabad & Mumbai at Early morning. Overnight Train journey.
Day 02 :    THRISSUR TO GURUVAYOOR (30 km / Total 1 hrs ) - (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   On arrival at Thrissur railway Station drive to Guruvayoor (1 Hrs). check into hotel. After freshup visit Famous Shree krishan Temple ("Dwarkas of South India") & Elephant Farm. Overnight Guruvayoor.
Day 03 :    GURUVAYOOR TO ALLEPPEY (140 km / Total 4 - 5 hrs ) (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   Early and after Breakfast, proceed to Alleppey (2 Hrs). On arrival check into hotel. After lunch enjoy famous Backwater Cruise Ride in Vembanad Lake.(at Your Own Coast), . Overnight Alleppey.
Day 04 :    ALLEPPEY TO TRIVENDRUM (150 km) (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   Morning After Breakfast enjoy famous Backwater Cruise Ride in Vembanad Lake.(at Your Own Coast), After Ride and Lunch Proceed for Trivendrum (5 Hrs), Arrive Trivendrum at late Evening. Overnight Trivendrum.
Day 05 :    KOVALAM & TRIVENDRUM SIGHTSEEING (30 KM Total) (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   After breakfast visit Padmanabh Swamy Temple. After Lunch drive to Kovalam Beach, Eenjoy on Beach, and water sport Activitice. Late Night back to Hotel. Overnioght Trivendrum.
Day 06 :    TRIVENDRUM - KANYAKUMARI  (85 KM / 2 Hrs)
   After breakfast drive to Kanyakumari, arrive & transfar in Hotel. After lunch Visit to Gandhi Mandap, Triveni Sangam Ghat & vivekananda Rock memorial. Overnight Kanyakumari.
Day 07 :    KANYAKUMARI - RAMESHWAR  (310 KM / 9 - 10 Hrs) (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   After breakfast drive to Rameshwar, Enroute visit Pamban bridge, APJ Abdulkalam Memorial, Indira Gandhi Road Bridge. arrive & transfar to Hotel. Evening Free time for Shopping. . Overnight Rameshwer.
Day 08 :    RAMESHWAR (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   Morning to Afternoon free Time for Sri Rameshwar Main Temple Darshan - Which is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of India & Agnitirtham Snan. After Lunch Visit Dhanushkhori & Abdul Kalam House. (at Your Own Cost) . Overnight Rameshwaram..
Day 09 :    RAMESHWAR - MADURAI - THEKKADY (325 Km / 10 - 11 Hrs With Madurai Darshan)  (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   Early & after Breakfast morning 6.00am proceed for Thekkady, Enroute Visit & Darshan Minaxi Temple In Madurai (Madurai Minaxi Temple is 12.30pm To 16.00pm Remains Closed), Arrive Thekkady at late night. transfar to hotel. Overnight Thekkady.
Day 10 :    THEKKADY (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   Morning After Breakfast Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary by boat in Periyar Lake (At your Own Coast). After lunch Evening Enjoy Kalaripayattu-the oldest of martial arts Show and Kathakali Show (At Your Own Cost) Overnight Thekkady.
Day 11 :    THEKKADY - MUNNAR (90 KM) (Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner)
   After breakfast drive to Munnar(2-3 Hrs) Enroute See Tata Tea Garden, Arrive Munnar at noon, transfer to Hotel. Rest of day free time for Shopping., Overnioght Munnar.
Day 12 :    MUNNAR
   Morning After Breakfast Enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam. View Tea Garden, visit Echo Point and Eravikulam (Rajamalai) National Park. Overnight Munnar.
Day 13 :    MUNNAR - ATHIRAPILLY WATER FALL - ERNAKULAM RAILWAY STATION DROP  (200 KM)
   After breakfast, drive to visit the Adi Shankara Sthoopam Temple In Kaladi & continue towards Ernakulam Railway Station to board your train for your home Destination. If Time is remaining we will be visit asia biggest Lulu Mall in cochin for shopping. Overnight Train Journey. (Breakfast || Lunch & Parcel Light Dinner ( Puri - Bhaji) )
Day 14 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI ( Only Lunch & Dinner From Train Pentry Car)
   Arrive Mumbai at evening. TOUR END with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passenger arriving by air has to transfer to airport at their own expense.
