Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    BHUBNESHWER AIRPORT TO PURI (60 KM) OR ARRIVE PURI RAILWAY STATION
   Arrive Bhubneshwer Airport and Drive to Puri (60 KM - 2 Hrs) . OR Arrive Puri Railway Station and Transfer to Hotel, Evening visit of lord jagannath temple (non hindus are not allowed) / relax on the golden beach of puri. Overnight at Puri. 
Day 02 :    PURI - CHILKA LAKE - PURI
   Morning after leisure breakfast Konark Sun Temple – A World Haritage Site After visit back to hotel , After Lunch Drive to chilka lake the largest brackish water body in asia & is the home of numerous birds & sea life. Visit to island by boat for bird watching & also sea irawadi dolphins & sea mouth (meeting point of sea & lake name as chilika lagoon) back to the puri , Overnight Puri.
Day 03 :    PURI - BHUBNESHWER - HOWRAH
   After break-fast Check - Out From Hotel and proceed to Bhubaneshwar. En-route enjoy shopping at Pipli and visit Dhauli – it is presumed to be the area where Kalinga War was fought. See the famous Shanti Stupa and inscription carved by King Ashoka. and Also Visit to nandankanzoo ( closed on monday ).this zoo is famous for variety wild life sanctuary & also famous for the white tiger safari and botanical garden. After Dinnar Proceed for Howrah By 18410 Sri Janannath Exp. Train From Bhubneshwer at 23.50pm. Overnight Train Journey.
Day 04 :    ARRIVE HOWRAH
   Arrive Howrah and transfer to Hotels. After Lunch Free time for Rest and Shopping. Overnight Howrah
Day 05 :    KOLKOTTA - GANGA SAGAR - KOLKOTTA
   Early Morning 3.00am start for full day excursion to Gangasagar - An Island in the confluence of River Ganga & Bay of Bengal & famous for Kapil Muni Ashram which is considered to be a great religious significance. Evening return back to Kolkata. Overnight stay at hotel.
Note: We will provide vehicle for Kolkata to Ferry Ghat i.e. Lot No. 8 / Harwood Point (90 Km.) & back to Kolkata. After reaching Lot No. 8 you have to take the ferry to cross the Muriganga River for Kachuberia. And take private Bus / Jeep for Ganga Sagar Island & back Kachuberia. (Harwood Point To Kachuberia & Kachuberia To Back Harwood Point Ferry Charges. and Kachuberia to Ganga Sagar Island & back Kachuberia - Vehicle Charges.) ((Non A/C & SIC BASIC , Include In Tour Cost).
Day 06 :    KOLKOTTA LOCAL
   After Lunch Check -Out From Hotel & Visit Belur Math, Dakshineshwar Kali Temple, Victoria Memorial and pass by Howrah Bridge - The bridge is a famous symbol of Kolkata. City orientation includes Dalhousie Square, Fort Williams, Secretariat Building and Old Court House Street. Overnight Kolkotta
Day 07 :    KOLKOTTA - MUMBAI
   After Brekfast Drive to Kolkotta Airport and Fly to Mumbai, Arrive Mumbai . With memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Tour END
