Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI TO DEHRADUN (By Flight) TO MUSSOORIE  (85 KM)
   Depart for Dehradun By Flight From Mumbai at Early Morning. Arrive Dehradun Airport and proceed to Mussoorie (1 hrs.). Evening visit Mall Road for shopping. Overnight Mussoorie.
Day 02 :    MUSSOORIE
   After breakfast we will visit the famous Kempty Water Falls by Cable Car (at Your Own cost). After lunch visit Mussoorie Lake. Night halt at Mussoorie.
Day 03 :    MUSSOORIE - HARIDWAR (85 KM)
   After Breakfast drive to Haridwar (3 - 4 Hrs), Enroute visit Rishikesh Sightseen, After Arrive Haridwar transfer to Hotel. Evening Visit Haridwar Ghat to Enjoy Har ki Pouri Ganga Arty. Rest of time free for Shopping. Overnight Haridwar.
Day 04 :    HARIDWAR - NAINITAL (235 KM)
   After breakfast proceed to Nainital.(10 - 12 hrs journey) On arrival day will be free at leisure. Night halt at Nainital 
Day 05 :    NAINITAL - RANIKHET - NAINITAL
   After lunch full day sightseeing Tour of Ranikhet. Evening return back to hotel. Night stay at Nainital.
Day 06 :    NAINITAL
   After breakfast excursion of Nainital Lake Tour which includes Naini Lake, Sattal, Navkuchiya Tal, Bhimtal etc (Boating at your own cost). After lunch local sightseeing of Naini Lake, cable car (at your own cost). In the evening free time for photography / shopping. Night halt at Nainital.
Day 07 :    NAINITAL - JIM CORBET (RAMNAGAR) (65 KM)
   Early morning check out hotel and enjoy the magnificent view of Sunrise and then proceed to Corbett Park (7 - 8 hrs. journey). In the evening enjoy the natural scenic beauty of this exotic place. Night halt at Corbett
Day 08 :    JIM CORBET (RAMNAGAR)
   After breakfast we will start the Jungle Safari from Seetavani / Jirna Gates (at Your Own cost). Evening is free for rest. Night halt at Corbett Park ( Ramnagar).
Day 09 :    CORBET - DELHI (268 KM) TO MUMBAI
   After & Early Breakfast morning 5.00am Drive to Delhi Railway Station (8-9 Hrs) and proceed for Mumbai by Durento Exp. Train From New Delhi Railway Station. . Tour End With Memoreble mamories With Kanaiya Travels.
Day 10 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai Railway Station.at Evening 16.30 Pm. TourEnd With Memoreble mamories With Shree Kanaiya Travels.
