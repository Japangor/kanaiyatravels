Days Tour Detail
Day 1 :     
   Depart for Delhi By Train from mumbai at morning. Overnight Train Journey.
Day 2 :    DELHI - NAINITAL (BHIMTAL) (300 KM)
   Arrive Delhi Railway Station & Proceed for Nainital (Bhimtal) (11 - 12 Hrs) By 42 Seater 2x2 OR 3X2 Cotch.Light Lunch at On the Route. Arrive Bhimtal at late Evening Transfer to Hotel. (Parking To Hotel Passenger By Walk). Overnight Nainital (Bhimtal).
Day 3 :    BHIMTAL (NAINITAL)
   After breakfast excursion of Nainital Lake Tour which includes Naini Lake, Sattal, Navkuchiya Tal, Bhimtal etc . After lunch local sightseeing of Naini Lake, cable car. In the evening free time for photography / shopping. Night halt at Nainital(Bhimtal).
(Boating and Texi Fare at Your Own Cost).
Day 4 :    NAINITAL (BHIMTAL) - RANIKHET - NAINITAL (110 KM RETURN)
   After lunch full day sightseeing Tour of Ranikhet. Evening return back to hotel. Night stay at Nainital. (Bhimtal).
Day 5 :    BHIMTAL (NAINITAL) - CORBET PARK ( RAMNAGAR) (90 KM)
   Early morning check out hotel and enjoy the magnificent view of Sunrise and then proceed to Corbett Park (3 hrs. journey) (Breakfast be provided during journey). In the evening enjoy the natural scenic beauty of this exotic place. Night halt at Corbett Park (Ramnagar).
Day 6 :    CORBET PARK ( RAMNAGAR) - JUNGLE SAFARI - RISHIKESH (200 KM)
   Early Morning 6.00am Enjoy the Jungle Safari from Seetavani / Jirna Gates (Rs. 650 Per Person at Your Own cost). After Breakfast Check out From Hotel and Proceed To Rishikesh (8-9 Hrs). Overnight Rishikesh.
Day 7 :    RISHIKESH
   Full Day free time Enjoy for Local activities & Adventure Sports. (like -- River Rafting (Rs.400 to Rs.1300 Per PersonDepending upon the Distance), Bungee Jumping (Rs.3500 Per Person), Flying Fox (Rs.1700 – Rs.3000 Per Person), Giant Swing, (Rs.3000 Per Person), Cliff Jumping) Overnight Rishikesh.
Day 8 :    RISHIKESH - DELHI
   Early & After breakfast we will Proceed for Delhi (7 - 8 Hrs), after arrive transfer to Hotel , Rest of Day Free time for Visit Lotus Temple and Kutumb Minar. Overnight Delhi
Day 9 :    DROP TO DELHI RAILWAY STATION.
   Morning After Breakfast Check out from Hotel & Visit Delhi Local Sightseeing (Like - Akshardham Temple, Metro, Red Fort, National Gandhi Museum , Gandhi Smriti , India Gate, Etc, After Light Dinner Drop to Delhi Railway Station To Board your train for your return journey. Overnight Train Journey..
Day 10 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai at late Evening. Tour End with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
