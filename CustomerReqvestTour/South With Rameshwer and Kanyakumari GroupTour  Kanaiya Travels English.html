Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI - KANYAKUMARI ( 3AC Train Journey)
   All the tour participants are requested to report  01 hr prior to the journey at Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminal to board Train no 16339 Nagercoil Exp. 20:30 PM Departure to Nagercoil Junction. Overnight train journey.
Day 02 :    TRAIN JOURNEY
   Full Day & Night Train journey.
Day 03 :    NAGERCOIL - KANYAKUMARI  (15 KM / 25 MINITs)
   Arrive Nagercoil at morning 10.30am and proceed for kanyakumari (25 Minit). Arrive Kanyakumari and Transfer to Hotel. After Fresh - Up visit Vivekanand Memorial Rock, Triveni Sangam, Kanyakumari Mata Mandir & Gandhi Memorial etc. Evening enjoy sunset at beach. .. Overnight Kanyakumari.
Day 04 :    KANYAKUMARI - RAMESHWAR  (310 KM)
   Early in the morning we can see Sunrise from the terrace of the hotel. After Breakfast Proceed to Rameswaram (7 - 8 Hrs) and on the way Annai Indhira Gandhi Bomban Bridge and bamban rail Scissors Bridge After arrive transfar to Hotel. Overnight Rameshwar.
Day 05 :    RAMESHWAR
   Morning Free Time for Rameshwer Temple Darshan After lunch visit Dhanushkodi (at Your Own Cost) which certainly is one of the best things to see and do in Rameshwaram. Dhanushkodi, in 1964 was completely washed away by the cyclone but it is still a very popular spot to visit. It is nothing but a strip of land about one km wide and 18 km long on the eastern end of Rameswaram island which divides the waters of the Indian Ocean on one side and on the other the waters of the Bay of Bengal. The two seas otherwise known as Ratnakaran and Mahodadhi with their confluence is shaped like a bow while the strip of land resemble an arrow poised for release. Devout Hindus consider the Adi Sethu or the arrowhead, a sacred place to perform religious rites. It is also place worth visiting for its scenic beauty and bird life. Over night Rameshwar.
Day 06 :    RAMESHWAR - MADURAI - - KODAIKENAL (287 KM)
   After breakfast proceed to Kodaikenal. Enroute visit and Darshan Minaxi Temple in Madurai, Arrive Kodaikenal Late evening. Overnight Kodaikenal. 
Day 07 :    KODAIKENAL
   After breakfast visit Pillar Rock, Green valley view, Silver Cascade water falls and Upper Lake View. After lunch free for leisure activities. Overnight Kodaikenal.
Day 08 :    KODAIKENAL - OOTY (TOTAL 252 KM)
   AfterBreakfast proceed for to "queen of hill station" ooty (8 - 9 Hrs). Arrive at late evening. Transfar to Hotel. Overnight t Ooty.
Day 09 :    OOTY
   After breakfast, proceed to Coonoor by Heritage Toy Train. Cab will reach by road and take you for Coonoor Sightseeing - Sim's Park, Lamb's Rock, Dolphin Nose, Tea factory, Tea Museum and Doddabetta Peak. Later in Ooty - Rose Garden & Botanical Garden.. Evening Free time for Boating. Overnight Ooty.
Day 10 :    OOTY - MYSORE (125 KM)
   Drive to Mysore after breakfast (4 - 5 hours). Along the way visit Mudumalai Forest or Bandipur Forest and enjoy a jungle safari (at your own expense). On arrival in Mysore in the evening visit Vrindavan Gardens as well as enjoy the world famous beautiful view of the Grand Maharaja Palace illuminated by 65,000 bulbs at night. Overnight stay in Mysore.
Day 11 :    MYSORE TO BANGLORE ( 145 km / 4 - 5 Hrs )
   After breakfast visit Mysore Zoo and Maharaja Palace, Chamundi Hills and Nandi, check out from the hotel after lunch and leave for Bangalore (145 km / 4 - 5 hours). Visit Bull Temple and Indira Gandhi Musical Fountain on arrival in Bangalore in the evening. Overnight stay in Bangalore. 
Day 12 :    BANGLORE TO MUMBAI (3 AC Train Journey)
   After breakfast in the morning, check out from the hotel and visit various tourist destinations of Bangalore as per the convenience of the time. Departure to Mumbai by 11302 Udayan Express train departing from KSR Bangalore railway station with light partial meal at late evening. Night train travel.
Day 13 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrival in Mumbai at 20.15 pm. The tour ended with a memorable experience organized by Shree Kanaiya Travels.
