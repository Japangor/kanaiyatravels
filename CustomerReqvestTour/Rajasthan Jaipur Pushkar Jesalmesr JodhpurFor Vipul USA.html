Days Tour Detail
23 Jan. :    MUMBAI TO JAIPUR
   Depart for Jaipur by 12955 MMCT - JP Super fast OR 12979 BDTS - JP Superfast Train From Surat at Evening 21.00pm. Overnight Train Journey.
24 Jan. :    ARRIVE JAIPUR
   Arrive Jaipur and transfar to Hotel. After lunch, we will depart for sightseeing. The places you will be visiting in Jaipur are: City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Pink City Walls, and Jantar Mantar. Back to hotel and Night stay at Jaipur.
25 Jan. :    JAIPUR
   In the morning After breakfast visit  Amer Fort. After Lunch visit Iskon Temple & Birla Temple. Evening free time for Visit Chokhi Dhani - a traditional Rajasthani village With Dinner (at Your Own Cost). Overnight Jaipur.
26 Jan. :    JAIPUR - AJMER - PUSHKAR (150 KM)
   After Breakfast Proceed to Pushkar(3 Hrs), en-route visit Moinuddin Chishti Darga Arrive Pushker, . Evening Free time for Visit Bramha Temple & Many Hindu Temple near by Pushkar Lake. Overnight at Pushkar.
27 Jan. :    PUSHKAR - JODHPUR (185 KM - 4 to 5 Hrs)
   Proceed to Jodhpur (4 to 5 Hrs) On arrival transfar to hotel. Evening free time in Jodhpur for shopping. Overnight Jodhpur.
28 Jan. :    JODHPUR
   Enjoy the city Tour of Jodhpur. Visit Ummaid Bhavan Palace museum, Mehrangarh Fort which includes Moti Mahal, Phool Mahal, Sheesh Mahal, Silah Khana, Daulat Khana and Rang Mahal. Later visit Jaswant Thada. Overnight Jodhpur.
29 Jan. :    JODHPUR TO JAISELMER (281 km / 5 hrs)
   After & Early breakfast proceed for Jaisalmer, Enroute visit Ramdevra (Juna Ranuja) Samdhi Temple & Pokran, Arrive and transfar to hotel. Evening visit Patwan ki Haweli, and Nathmalaji ki Haweli. Overnight Jaisalmer
30 Jan. :    JAISELMER - SAND DUNES (55 KM / 1 Hrs)
   Enjoy city Tour of Jaisalmer. Visit Golden Fort, and museum in the morning. After Lunch proceed for Sand Dunes, On arrive Enjoy Camel ride and Indo - Pak Border, Transfar to Hotel. Late Evening Enjoy Folk dance and magnificent sunset in famous Sam Sand Dunes . Overnight Sand Dunes.
31 Jan. :    SAND DUNES TO JODHPUR TO SURAT (By AC Train)
   After Breakfast, Drive to Jodhpur Railway Station ( 300km - 7 Hrs) and proceed for Surat By 19056 - JU - BL Exp. OR 12479 - Surya Nagari Exp. Train at Evening 19.00pm. Overnight Train Journey.
01 Feb. :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Surat at morning 07.00. Tour End With Memoreble mamories With Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
