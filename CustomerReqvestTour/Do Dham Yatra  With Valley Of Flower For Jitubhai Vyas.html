Sept. 03 :    SITAPUR - GOURIKUND - KEDARNATH - GOURIKUND - SITAPUR
(Total 8 km By Road & 32 km Trek Return)
   Early Morning drive to Gaurikund, Trek start from Gaurikund to Kedarnath by Pony / Doli (At Your Own Cost). After darshan / Pooja at Temple, return to Sitapur at Same Route. Pilgrims going by Helicopter (Cost = Rs. 5500 To 6000/- Per Person): Early morning proceed to Sirsi / Phata / Guptakashi Helipad to Kedarnath Helipad. Later, Proceed to Kedarnath Temple by walk (1 Km.). After darshan / Pooja at Temple, return to Helipad to board the Helicopter back to Sitapur. Overnight Sitapur.
Sept. 04 :    SITAPUR  - PIPALKOTI
   Early morning, Can Intrested are Visit Triguni Narayani Temple (At Your Extra Cost.) Later, drive to pipalkoti via Guptkashi . pipalkoti is small and scenic town situated at an elevation of 1260 mts above sea level. Overnight stay Pipalkoti.
Sept. 05 :    PIPALKOTI - BADRINATH (80 KM)
   Drive to Badrinath .Arrive. at late evening visit Badrinath temple for Aarti. Rest Of Day free for Visit Mana Village (at Extra Cost). Overnight Badrinath.
Sept. 06 :   

BADRINATH - GOVINDGHAT - GHANGHARIA ( 23 KMS DRIVE + 14 KMS TREK )
   Morning After Breakfast drive to Govindghat (1 - 2 Hrs). Upon arrival Govindghat stat trek towards Ghangaria (14 KM). Evening arrival Ghangaria check in at Camp for overnight stay.Overnight Ghangharia.
(Lunch || Dinner and Night Stay at your own cost).
Sept. 07 :    GHANGHARIA - VALLEY OF FLOWERS - GHANGHARIA  (10 KM Return )
   After breakfast trek to Valley Of Flowers (3858 m) from Ghangaria. The valley is an enchanting sight with an impressive array of rare wildflowers like Geranium, Marsh marigold, Prinula, Potentilla, Geum, Asters, Lilium, Ranunculus, Corydalis, Inula, Braham kamal, Campanula, Pedicularis, Arisaema, Morina, Impatiens, Bistorta, Ligularia, Anaphalis, Saxifrages, Sibbaldia, Thermo- psis, Trollius, and numerous others. Most of the flowers have medicinal values too. The valley is 10 kms long, 2 kms wide, and is divided by the Pushpawati stream, into which several tiny streams and waterfalls merge. After enjoying the beauty of valley trek back to Ghangaria. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel. Overnight Ghangharia.
(Breakfast || Lunch || Dinner and Night Stay at your own cost).
Sept. 08 :    GHANGHARIA - GOVINDGHAT - RUDRAPRAYAG (14 KMS TREK + 130 KMS DRIVE)
   Morning t trek down to Govindghat upon arrival Govindghat driver to Rudraprayag. En-route visit Nandpryag, On arrival Rudraprayag check in at hotel, Overnight Rudraprayag. (Breakfast at your own cost).
Sept. 09 :    RUDRAPRAYAG - RISHIKESH - HARIDWAR  (165 KM)
   Morning After Breakfast Proceed for Haridwar.(06 - 07 Hrs), Overnight Haridwar.
Sept. 10 :    DROP TO HARIDWAR RAILWAY STATION  (5 KM)
   After Lunch Checkout From Hotel and leave for Haridwar Railway Station to board Your Train for Mumbai. Tour End.
