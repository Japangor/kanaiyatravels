Days Tour Detail
25 April :    MUMBAI - DELHI
   Depart For Delhi By 22209 Mumbai - New Delhi Duronto Exp. From Mumbai Central at 23.00pm. Overnight Train Journey.
26 April :    DELHI - JAMMU
   Arrive Delhi at Evening 16.30pm & After Dinner Proceed for Jammu By Next Train ( Min. 3 Hrs Break). Overnight Train Journey.
27 April :    JAMMU - KATRA (45 KM )
   Arrive Jammu at Morning and . Proceed to Katra (2 Hrs). After arrive Transfer to Hotel (Min. 2 -3 Hrs Wait For Room Alloted- Check In Time is 12.00). After Lunch can intrested are start for Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra by Horse (Rs. 2000/- ), Dolly (Rs. 3500 To 4000/-) OR Walk(Free) (Return 28 km), (Also avaliable Helicopter Sarvice for Darshan (Only Day Time), Helicopter droped 3 km before Temple, Book Your Helicopter Ticket Directly from www.maavaishnodevi.org . Overnight Katra.
28 April :    KATRA
   Full Day Free For Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan. Overnight Katra.
29 April :    KATRA - SRINAGAR (270 KM RETURN)
   Very Early Morning 6.00am After Breakfast Proceed for Srinagar (12 to 13 Hrs), Enroute visit & lunch at Patnitop, Enroute pass asia largest Tunel at Banihal, Anantnag. Avantipora. Arrive Srinagar at Late Evening. Overnight Srinagar. 
30 April :    SRINAGAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEN (TOTAL 25 KM)
   Early Morning After Tea visit Sri Shankaracharya Temple.(1100 ft. above surface level of the main city on the hill) Climb 270 steps to reach the temple from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Srinagar. Return Hotel After breakfast visit Moghul Gardens i.e. Chashmeshahi - The Royal Spring, Nishat Baug - Garden of Bliss and Shalimar - Garden of Love. Evening Enjoy Shikara Ride (At Your Own Cost). covering Nehru Park, Charchinar and Floating Market. Overnight Srinagar.
01 May :    SRINAGAR - SONMARG - KHIR BHAWANI - SRINAGAR  ( 170 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast full day trip to Sonmarg (Subject to Military Permission), which is located at 85 kms from Srinagar at 2740 mtrs. above Sea level. Sonmarg means meadow of gold Sonmarg is the home of many beautiful & serene lakes. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point . Also visit Thajiwas By Horse . Evening return back to Srinagar. Enroute visit Khir Bhavani Temple and Hazaratbal Dargah.. Overnight Srinagar
02 May :    SRINAGAR - GULMARG - SRINAGAR (130 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg (2-3 Hrs)(9,500 ft. above sea level). En route pick up overcoats, gloves and boots, available on hire (optional), at Tangmarg. From Gulmarg take Gandola Ropeway (At Your Own Coast) . Have fun at the Snow Point.. Evening back Srinagar. Enroute visit & Shopping .Apple & Dryfruit From Firm. Overnight Srinagar . (please be aware, here you can be cheated by horse riders)
03 May :    SRINAGAR - SAFRON FIRM - AVANTIPORE - PAHELGAM (90 KM )
   After breakfast proceed for Pahalgam (3-4 Hrs), Enroute visit Safron Firm, Cricket Bat Factory, Avantipora Museam, Maturn Tirth. Also Enroute Enjoy Rever Rafting. Arrive pahalgam late afternoon. transfer to Hotel. After lunch day is free for rest / shopping / Photographi. Overnight Pahalgam.
04 May :    PAHELGAM 
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley (Include in Tour cost) the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley. Enjoy horse riding at this beautiful place, Evening is free for shopping / rest.Overnight Pahalgam..Overnight Pahelgam.
05 May :    PAHALGAM - UDHAMPUR  (230 KM)
   After breakfast we proceed to Udhampur Railway Station (8-9 Hrs) OR Jammu station (12 -13 Hrs. journey) to board Your Train For Delhi. Enroute Visit Patnitop. Lunch at Patnitop. Overnight Train Journey.
06 May :    DELHI - AKSHARDHAM TEMPLE - MUMBAI
   Arrive Delhi morning and Proceed for Akshardham Temple Gate No. 4 For Fresh -up. ( Common Bathroom). After Freshup & Dry Breakfast By walk go to Gate No.1 and Visit Akshardham Temple. After Lunch Proceed for Nizamuddin Railway Station to Board your Train 12954 Aug. Kranti Rajdhani Exp. at 16.40pm. Overnight Train Journey.
07 May :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   After Breakfast In Train arrive Borivali at Morning 09.00am. . Tour End With Memoreble Memories With Kutumb Milan Group and Kanaiya Travels.
