Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI TO KATRA
   Depart for katra by 12471 swaraj Exp train from bandra terminal at 11.00am. Overnight Train Journey.
Day 02 :    ARRIVE KATRA
   Tour participants are requested to report Kanaiya Travels ' Manager, on arrival at Katra Railway Station by any Train. passengers arriving by Jammu any other Train / Air has to reach hotel directly by their own Coast. After Dinner can intrested are start for Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra by Horse(Rs. 1800/- ), Dolly (Rs. 4000/-) OR Walk(Free) (Return 28 km), (Also avaliable Helicopter Sarvice for Darshan (Only Day Time), Helicopter droped 3 km before Temple, Book Your Helicopter Ticket Directly from www.maavaishnodevi.org ). Overnight Katra.
Day 03 :    KATRA
   Full Day Free For Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan. Overnight Katra.
Day 04 :    KATRA - SRINAGAR
   Early in the morning we proceed towards srinagar by Passing Nazari & Banihal Tunnel, Pira ( Famous for Kashmiri Rajma - Rice - Pure Ghee & Anar Dana Chatney ), Anantnag. Arrive Srinagar at late Evening. Transfar in Hotel. Overnight Srinagar.
Day 05 :    SRINAGAR - BALTAL
   After early breakfast proceed for Baltal (3-4 Hrs)(Base camp for Sree Amarnath Yatra). Enroute visit Sonmarg Glaciear. After arrive transfer to Baltal Tent. Overnight Baltal
Day 06 :    BALTAL - AMARNATH CAVES - BALTAL
   Early morning 3.00 am Start for Shree Amarnath Yatra by Walk , Horse , Dolly After Darshan - Pooja Return Proceed for Other Route Baltal by Walk , Horse , Dolly (13KM - 6 Hrs). Arrive Baltal Transfer to Tent. Overnight Baltal Tent.
Day 07 :    BALTAL - SRINAGAR
   Early morning after breakfast drive to Srinagar (3 Hrs). Enroute Visit Sonmarg. Arrive Srinagar Transfer to Hotel. Rest Of day Free for Shikara Ride & Shopping.Overnight Srinagar.
Day 08 :    SRINAGAR - KATRA
   Early Morning Proceed for Katra (10 -11 Hrs), Arrive Katra at Late Evening, Rest Of day free for Rest. Overnight Katra..
Day 09 :    KATRA - SHIVKHORI - KATRA (170 KM Return)
   After breakfast Drive to Shivkhori (3 Hrs), After Arrive Shivkhori Darshan For God Shiv By Walk or Horse Or Dolly 9 Km Return, After Darshan pooja back Katra. Overnight at Katra.
Day 10 :    KATRA TO MUMBAI
   Early and after breakfast Drop to Katra Railway Station to Board Your Train For Mumbai / Surat / Vadodara / Ahmedabad. Passengers travelling by air has to reach Jammu airport at their own expense. 
Day 11 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai at evening & Tour End with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.  Tour End.
