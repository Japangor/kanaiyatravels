   After breakfast Drive to Omkaleshwer(4 Hrs). Visit Omkaleshwar Jyotirling, Mamleshwar mahadev & Shani Mandir. Return to Ujjain and Night at Ujjain Hotel.

   Early Morning 3.00am Visit ( By Auto Riksha - Own Cost) Mahakaleshwara temple darshan With Bhasma Arty, After Breakfast visit Sandipani Ashram, Sidhavat Temple, Mangalnath Temple, Gadkalika Temple, Bharthari Gufa, Harrisiddhi Mata, Kaal Bhairav Temple. Evening Transfar to Ujjain Railway Station and proceed for Mumbai by 12962 Avantika Exp. Train - Departure at 18.55pm from Ujjain. Overnight train Journey.
Day 05 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI - ( NO ANY FOOD SERVICE )
    Arrive Mumbai at early morning 6.40am. Tour End
