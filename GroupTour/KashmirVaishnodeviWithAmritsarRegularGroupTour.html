Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    ARRIVE AMRITSAR
   Pickup From Amritsar Railway Station & transfer to hotel, Passenger's arriving by Air has to reach hotel at their own. Nihgthalt at Amritsar.
Day 02 :    AMRITSAR SIGHTSEEING & NIGHT AMRITSAR TO JAMMU ( BY TRAIN)
   After breakfast sightseeing Tour of Jalianwala Baug, Golden Temple (By Auto Riksha at Your Own Cost). After lunch, checkout from hotel and drive to Wagah Border (Include in Tour Cost) for visit the famous for military parade (Flag march) by India and Pakistan's Army.. Night After Dinner proceed to Jammu by Non AC Sleeper Train OR Bus. Overnight Train / Bus Journey.
Day 03 :    JAMMU TO PAHALGAM (265 KM - 8 - 9 Hrs )
   Morning arrival in Jammu and proceed to Pahalgam. On the way visit Nasri Tunnel (Asia's largest tunnel), Pidah (famous for Rajma-rice and Pomegranate Dana Chutney), Banihal Tunnel and Anantnag Reach Pahalgam late evening. Night stay in Pahalgam.
Day 04 :    PAHALGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley(at Your Owen Cost). After Lunch Visit Kashmir Vally ,Dabyan , Baisaran , By horse riding Evening Free time for Photographi at Lidder River, Nighthalt at Pahelgam.
Day 05 :    PAHALGAM - SRINAGAR ( 90 KM )
   After breakfast proceed for Srinagar (3-4 Hrs). Enroute visit, Cricket bat Factory and Safron Firm. Arrive Srinagar & transfer to Hotel , Evening Enjoy Shikara Ride. Nighthal at Srinagar.
Day 06 :    SRINAGAR - GULMARG - SRINAGAR ( 130 KM RETURN )
   After breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg (3 Hrs - 9,500 ft. above sea level). En route pick up overcoats, gloves and boots, available on hire (optional), at Tangmarg. From Gulmarg take Gandola Ropeway (At Your Own Coast) for Khilanmarg (10,050ft. above the sea level). Have fun at the Snow Point.. Evening back Srinagar.. Nighthal at Srinagar.
Day 07 :    SRINAGAR - SONMARG - KHIR BHAWANI - SRINAGAR  ( 170 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast full day trip to Sonmarg, which is located at 85 kms from Srinagar Sonmarg means meadow of gold Sonmarg is the home of many beautiful & serene lakes. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point. Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass (Subject to Military Permission) OR Thajiwas By Local Texi .(at your own cost), Evening return back to Srinagar. Enroute Enjoy River Rafting. Nighthal at Srinagar.
Day 08 :    SRINAGAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING
   Early morning visit Shankaracharya Temple.(1100 ft. above surface level of the main city on the hill) Climb 270 steps to reach the temple from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Srinagar. After breakfast visit Tulip Garden (If Open), Moghul Gardens i.e. Chashmeshahi - The Royal Spring, Nishat Baug - Garden of Bliss and Shalimar - Garden of Love. Enjoy Shikara Ride. covering Nehru Park, Mina Bazar. . Nighthal at Srinagar.
Day 09 :    SRINAGAR TO KATRA (SHREE MATA VAISHNODEVI) (240 KM - 8 - 9 Hrs)
   Early Morning 6.00 am After Breakfast Proceed for Katra (8 Hrs). Arrive katra at evening and Transfer to Hotel. After Dinner can intrested are start for Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra by Horse, Dolly OR Walk (Return 28 km, (Also avaliable Helicopter Sarvice for Darshan (Only Day Time), Book Your Helicopter Ticket Directly from www.maavaishnodevi.or). Nighthalt at Katra.
Day 10 :    KATRA
   Full Day Free For Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan. Nighthalt at Katra.
Day 11 :    DROP TO KATRA RAILWAY STATION.
   After breakfast Drop to Katra Railway station to board Your Train for your home destination. TOUR END with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passengers travelling by air has reach to Jammu Airport at their own cost.
