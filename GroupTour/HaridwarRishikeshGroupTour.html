Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI TO HARIDWAR (Train Journey)
   Depart to Haridwar from Mumbai By Train at Afternoon 13.05pm.. Overnight at Train Journey.
Day 02 :    ARRIVE HARIDWAR
   Arrive Dev Bhumi Haridwar & transfer to Hotel / Dharamsala. Evening free time for enjoy famous ganga aarti & ganga snan on har ki pauri. Overnight at Haridwar.
Day 03 :    HARIDWAR - RISHIKESH - HARIDWAR (Return 60 km) (Auto Fare Include in Tour Cost)
   After breakfast full day sightseeing of Rishikesh (By auto Riksha - Auto Fare Include in Tour Cost), Visit Neelkanth Mahadev, Laxmanjhula, Lakhman Temple, Ramjhula, Triveni Ghat, Geeta Bhavan, Tera Manzil Temple & Parmarth Niketan Ashram. Return to Haridwar at Evening. Overnight at Haridwar.
Day 04 :    HARIDWAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING - (Ropway cable car Ticket & Auto Fare Include in Tour Cost)
   After breakfast Visit Mansa Devi Temple & Chandi Devi Temple By ropway. After lunch Visit Daksh Temple, Pawan Dham, Vaishnavdevi Temple, Bharat Mata Mandir & Shantikunj Gayatri Temple (By Auto Riksha) -. Evening Enjoy famous Ganga Aarti on Har Ki Pauri. Overnight at Haridwar. (Ropway cable car Ticket & Auto Fare Include in Tour Cost)
Day 05 :    HARIDWAR FULL DAY FREE
   Full Day free time for Bhajan - Pooja path OR Visit Massoorie OR Hastinapur (at your own expenses). Overnight at Haridwar.
Day 06 :    HARIDWAR - MUMBAI ( Train Journey)
   Morning to afternoon free time for shopping haridwar market or visit other temples. After lunch, transfar to Haridwar railway station to board your train for return journey. Overnight at Train Journey.
Day 07 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Arrive Mumbai at night. Tour End with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. TOUR END
