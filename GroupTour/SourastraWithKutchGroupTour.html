Day 01 :    MUMBAI - AHEMDABAD - BOTAD
   Depart for Botad From Mumbai by Bhavnagar Exp. Train at night. Overnight Train Journey.
Day 02 :    BOTAD - SARANGPUR - CHOTILA - VIRPUR - JETPUR - JUNAGARH (250 KM)
   Pick Up From Botad Railway Station at Morning. and Drive to Sarangpur,. 15 minits After Fresh - Up Visit Sarangpur Temple and Proceed for Junagarh. Enroute Visit Chotila Chamunda Ji Temple, Gondal Swaminarayan Temple and Jalaram Temple in Virpur. Arrive Junagar at late Evening. Overnight Junagarh.
Day 03 :    JUNAGARH - SASAN GIR (50 KM)
   Morning Free Time for Girnar Darshan. After lunch Visit Uperkot Fort , Darbar hall measum . Ashok Rock Edict,, and Damodar kund, Evening drive to Sasan Gir National Park, Overnight Sasan Gir
Day 04 :    SASAN GIR - SOMNATH (50 KM)
   Enjoy Jungle Safari by Jeep at Sasan Gir at 6.00 am. After lunch drive to Somnath. After arrival transfer to hotel. In the evening attend the aarti and later watch the light and sound show. Overnight at Somnath.
Day 05 :    SOMNATH - PORBUNDER - HARSIDDHI MATA - DWARKA (240 KM)
   Morning Free time for Darshan & Pooja. After Breakfast Drive to Porbunder, Visit Kirti Mandir – the place where Gandhiji was born and Sudama Temple – The only Sudama Temple in the world. Later proceed to Dwarka Enroute Visit Harsiddhi Mata Temple. Arrive Dwarka Transfer to Hotel. Rest of Day free for Enjoy on Beach and Dwarkesh Temple Darshan. Overnight Dwarka.
Day 06 :    DWARKA - BET DWARKA - DWARKA (44 KM RETURN)
   Today Visit Dwarkadeesh Temple. Take a holy dip in Gomti river, later leave to visit Nageshwar Jyotirling, Gopi Talav, Bet Dwarka, and on way back do visit Rukmani Temple, evening visit other temples on coastal area, attend evening aarti at Dwarkadish temple. Overnight Dwarka
Day 07 :    DWARKA - ANJAR - BHUJ (410 KM)
   Early Morning 6.00am drive to Bhuj (10 Hrs), Enroute visit Anjar Jesal - Toral Samadhi. Arrive Bhuj at late Evening. Transfer to Hotel. Overnight Bhuj.
Day 08 :    BHUJ - NARAYAN SAROVER - MATA NO MUDH - BHUJ (310 KM)
   After Breakfast Full day sightseeing for Ashapura Mata no Madh , Lakhpat Fort, unique Gurudwara and a beautiful tomb of Gosh Muhammad, Narayan, Koteshwer Shiva Temple. Overnight Bhuj.
Day 09 :    BHUJ - WHITE RANN (BANNI AND PANCHAM REGIONS)
   Today Morning  proceed to visit Hodka Village, Enroute visit  of explore the Banni and Pachcham regions and the edge of the Great Rann of Kutch. Visit Dhordo village, Bhirandiyara village, Ludiya village and Khavda village. Later visit Kalo Dungar (Black Hill) Overnight Bhuj.
Day 10 :    BHUJ TO MANDVI TO GANDHIDHAM (160 km) TO MUMBAI
   After Breakfast visit Aina Mahal Palace, Prag Mahal, Museum. After lunch check out from hotel & drive to Gandhidham via Mandvi for visit vijay vilas palace. and Late Evening Proceed for Mumbai By Bhuj - Dadar Exp. From Gandhidham at late night, Overnight Train Journey.
Day 11 :    ARRIVE MUMBAI
   Aarrive Mumbai at Morning. Tour End with memorable experience of the Tour conducted by Shree Kanaiya Travels.
