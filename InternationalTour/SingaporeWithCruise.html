Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    Arrive Singapore- Evening Free
   Arrive at Singapore, one of the most enjoyable cities in South East Asia. Upon arrival at Singapore Airport, our local Tour representative will meet you near the Exit area and transfer you to the hotel. Check-in and relax. Rest of the day is at your own leisure. Take a stroll through the city streets in the evening, enjoy the vibrance of the city and relish its local delicacies.
Stay overnight at hotel in Singapore.
Day 02 :    Singapore- Onboard SuperStar Gemini
  

You can enjoy a cozy morning time in your bed as you have a relaxed morning today. Have a fill at morning breakfast and check out from the hotel by 1200 hrs. Enjoy leisure time in Singapore exploring nearby places until your pick up in evening from Hotel Lobby for Harbour Front Centre to board your cruise liner- SuperStar Gemini. Boarding starts 04 hrs prior to departure. After a warm welcome on board, explore and enjoy the various facilities offered.
Stay overnight onboard SuperStar Gemini. (Breakfast, Meals on cruise)
Day 03 :    Singapore- Onboard SuperStar Gemini
   Wake up to an exotic morning over the seas. Enjoy a bed tea while taking stunning sea views. The beauty of sunrise on sea is incredible. Refresh and enjoy a wide spread of breakfast on board. (Note: Your sailing routes depend on your departure dates.) Onboard, you can enjoy various entertainment activities, shopping and dining at in-house restaurants.
Stay overnight onboard SuperStar Gemini. (All Meals)
Day 04 :    Disembark from Cruise- Night Safari
   Enjoy another beautiful morning over the deep seas. Relish wide spread breakfast and enjoy the aura for a while longer before going back to the land. On your return to Singapore, disembark from the cruise and meet our local Tour representative for your transfer to hotel. Check-in and relax. In the evening, embark on a night safari Tour. The world's first wildlife park built for visits at night, the night safari offers guests the unique experience of exploring wildlife in a tropical jungle at night. Through the use of subtle lighting technique, guests are able to view over 1,000 nocturnal animals of 100 species in vast naturalistic habitats. Enjoy tram ride and animal show. Stay overnight at hotel in Singapore.
Day 05 :    Singapore City Tour
   After breakfast, embark on a guided city Tour of Singapore and enjoy the colonial buildings, government houses, Orchard Road, China Town, and Little India. In the afternoon, proceed on a Sentosa Twilight Tour which includes a one-way cable car ride, admission ticket and visits to the Underwater World, Merlion Tower, Dolphin Lagoon & Songs of the Seas. A slice of paradise, Sentosa offers a kaleidoscope of attractions and activities. Step afoot the island and be mesmerized by the breathtaking beauty of the lush greenery peppered with exciting attractions - it’s a themed attraction, tropical resort, Nature Park and heritage centre all rolled into one. Stay overnight at hotel. 
Day 06 :    Return Home with Wonderful Memories
   Morning have a fill at breakfast, pack your bags and check out from the hotel (1200 hrs). Today your vacation at Singapore ends and you get transferred to airport for your flight back to Mumbai. On your return, we would like to receive your feedback about the trip. We sincerely hope you had a great trip. Departure
