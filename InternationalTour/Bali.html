Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    MUMBAI – BALI
  

Assemble at Chattrapati Shivaji International Airport T2 Terminal, 3.5 hours prior to your departure to board your flight for Bali via Kualalumpur. Arrive Bali Ngurah Rai International Airport, also known as Denpasar International Airport, & Proceed towards Visa on Arrival / Immigration followed by Bag collection & Customs. After that you will be greeted by Local Friendly Guide . Bali Airport to hotel The Puri Nusa Dua takes approx 30 – 40 minutes by Road, Free time for rest / shopping or to explore near by areas on your own., Overnight at the hotel.
Day 02 :    BALI - FULL DAY KINTAMANI & UBUD TOUR
  

After English Breakfast at hotel, proceed for a Full Day Kintamani & Ubud Tour which includes magnificent view of Mount and Lake Batur with the smoky Agung Volcano in the background., Later the tour will move on to Celuk the center of gold and silver works, Mas the center of woodcarvings, Ubud the center of Balinese paintings., Then visit to the holy springs of Tirta Empul at tampaksiring. Overnight at the hotel.
Day 03 :    BALI - FULL DAY WATERSPORT & ULUWATU TEMPLE TOUR
  

After English Breakfast at hotel, proceed for a Full Day Water Sport which includes parasailing in tanjung benoa beach at nusa dua area with 1x banana boat (other activity on personal account)., and visi Turtle Island by Glass Bottom Bottom Boat, Later the tour will be moving on to Uluwatu temple a cliff edge temple at 95 meters above sea level, with fantastic views of the Indian Ocean., Overnight at the hotel.
Day 04 :    BALI – BACK HOME.
   After English breakfast, Check Out and proceed towards Airport to board your flight for Mumbai. Arrive Mumbai & End of Memorable Tour Conducted by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
