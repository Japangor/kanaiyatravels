Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    BANGKOK TO PATTAYA (THAILAND)
   Arrive Bangkok Suvarnabhumi International Airport & Proceed towards Immigration followed by Bag collection & Customs. Meet Kanaiya Travel Escorts at Arrivals. Bangkok Airport to Pattaya by Road (2 Hours Drive). Free time for rest / shopping in Pattaya. Evening Visit World Famous “Alcazar Show” performed by Beautiful Artist of Thailand with complimentary drink during show. Overnight at Hotel in Pattaya.
Day 02 :    PATTAYA - CORAL ISLAND - PATTAYA
   After Breakfast, Transfer To Coral Island, A Famous Beach In The Pattaya By Speed Boat, After Reaching There, Enjoy By Your Own Rides Like Paragliding, Parasailing, Banana Rides, Water Scooter, Boating, Under Sea Walks And Many More Leisure Activities Return at Noon, & Lunch. Evening After Transfer To The Indian Restaurant For Dinner After Dinner Drop to Walking Street for Shopping and Enjoy Thai Massage. Return Hotel at Your Own.. Overnight At Pattaya. .
Day 03 :    PATTAYA - SAFARI WORLD - MARINE PARK - GEM FACTORY - BANGKOK
   After Breakfast And Check Out From The Hotel, Transfer To The Coach For Move To Bangkok, Arrival At Bangkok Within 2.5 Hrs, On Route We Enjoy Full Day In Safari World, A Famous Zoo, Animal Show, Sea Lion Show, Stunt Show, Dolphin Show, James Bond Show, And Gem Factory. After That Check Inn At The Hotel. Night Transfer To The Indian Restaurant For Dinner. Overnight At Bangkok.
Day 04 :    BANGKOK TEMPLE Tour & SHOPPING
   After English breakfast. Visit Golden Buddha & Reclining Buddha, Afternoon Drop to Bangkok Famous Shopping area INDRA MARKET. Enjoy shopping at your own & come back to hotel at your time by local taxi at your own expense (As Coach Parking not allowed). Overnight At Bangkok.
Day 05 :    BANGKOK - KUALA LUMPUR - GENTING HIGHLANDS (MALAYSIA)
   After Early English breakfast, check out Bangkok Hotel & proceed towards Bangkok airport to board your flight to Kuala Lumpur, Arrive Kuala Lumpur airport & Proceed towards Genting Highlands by road (2 Hours) Enroute Visit Batu Caves for Darshan of Lord Murugan & Lord Karthik Temples., check into Hotel  Evening is free at Leisure to enjoy & try your luck at Casino. Enjoy Dinner at Indian Restaurant and Overnight at Genting
Day 06 :    GENTING HIGHLANDS
   After English Breakfast Visit Genting Snow World Visit First World Indoor Theme Park Unlimited Rides, Visit History of Genting Galleria Orientation Walking Tour Of Genting Highland , Enjoy Grand Night Show performed by Various International Artists.Overnight Genting.
Day 07 :    GENTING HIGHLANDS - KUALA LUMPUR
   Breakfast at hotel. Morning proceed for KL City Tour which includes visit to the National Monuments, Jamek Mosque, and House of Parliament, Kings Palace etc. Lastly we visit to K.L.Tower from where you can view the KL city. Indian Dinner at Indian Restaurant. .Overnight Kuala Lumpur.
Day 08 :    KUALA LUMPUR - SINGAPORE
   After breakfast at hotel will transfer you Kuala Lumpur to Singapore by coach will take 4-5 hours to reach. After Finishing Check in formalities at hotel (Check in time after 14.00 hrs)., In evening Evening Night Safari Tour where you will be seeing the different wild animals in the night. After that back to hotel Indian dinner at Indian Restaurant.. .Overnight in Singapore.
Day 09 :    SINGAPORE - SENTOSA ISLAND Tour
   After Breakfast, proceed for City Tour, which includes visit to The Singapore River, Botanical and Orchid Gardens, Singapore Gem Factory, Parliament House and City Hall. Etc. Famous Sentosa Island. Take a Cable Car from Mt. Faber. Visit the Wax Museum, Surrender Chamber, Merlion the symbol of Singapore. We visit to Under Water World etc. Then take Monorail ride across the Sentosa Island. In the evening enjoy The Musical Fountain show. Indian dinner at Indian Restaurant and Overnight in Singapore.
Day 10 :    SINGAPORE - UNIVERSAL STUDIO
   Breakfast, morning free for leisure., Opportunity to visit optional Universal Studio / Jurong Bird Park / Singapore Zoo (at Your Own Cost), Rest Of Day Free time for Shopping. Overnight in Singapore.
Day 11 :    SINGAPORE - MUMBAI
   After taking breakfast today, it's time to check out of from the hotel and be transferred to the airport departing from Singapore to Mumbai. And to say good buy to all your new Destinations Visited and is time for you to board your flight back to home  We hope you return back to your home with wonderful memories of your Holiday with Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
