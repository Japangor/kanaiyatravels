Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    BANGKOK TO PATTAYA (THAILAND)
   Arrive Bangkok Airport & Proceed towards Immigration followed by Bag collection & Customs. Meet Escorts at Arrivals. Bangkok Airport to Pattaya by Road (2 Hours Drive). Free time for rest / shopping in Pattaya. Evening Visit World Famous “Alcazar Show” performed by Beautiful Artist of Thailand with complimentary drink during show. Overnight at Hotel in Pattaya.
Day 02 :    PATTAYA - CORAL ISLAND - PATTAYA
   After Breakfast, Transfer To Coral Island, A Famous Beach In The Pattaya By Speed Boat, After Reaching There, Enjoy By Your Own Rides Like Paragliding, Parasailing, Banana Rides, Water Scooter, Boating, Under Sea Walks And Many More Leisure Activities Return at Noon, & Lunch. Evening After Transfer To The Indian Restaurant For Dinner After Dinner Drop to Walking Street for Shopping and Enjoy Thai Massage. Return Hotel at Your Own.. Overnight At Pattaya. .
Day 03 :    PATTAYA - SAFARI WORLD - MARINE PARK - GEM FACTORY - BANGKOK
   After Breakfast And Check Out From The Hotel, Transfer To The Coach For Move To Bangkok, Arrival At Bangkok Within 2.5 Hrs, On Route We Enjoy Full Day In Safari World, A Famous Zoo, Animal Show, Sea Lion Show, Stunt Show, Dolphin Show, James Bond Show, And Gem Factory. After That Check Inn Hotel. Night Transfer To The Indian Restaurant For Dinner. Overnight Bangkok.
Day 04 :    BANGKOK TEMPLE TOUR & SHOPPING
   After English breakfast. Visit Golden Buddha & Reclining Buddha, Afternoon Drop to Bangkok Famous Shopping area INDRA MARKET. Enjoy shopping at your own & come back to hotel at your time by local taxi at your own expense (As Coach Parking not allowed). Overnight At Bangkok.
Day 05 :    BANGKOK - KUALA LUMPUR (MALAYSIA)
   Check out & transfer to Suvarnabhumi International Airport, Bangkok. After Check in Procedures & Immigration board the flight departing to Kuala Lumpur. Arrive at Kuala Lumpur Airport. After Immigrations, Customs & Baggage collection transfer directly for Putrajaya Orientation. Later transfer to Kuala Lumpur City Tour including the view of famous Petronas Twin Tower, Entrance to K.L. Tower, Museum, Independence Square, Grand Palace etc. Later transfer to hotel (Check in time 1400 hrs). Free time to explore city at your own or shopping. Overnight at hotel in Kuala Lumpur.
Day 06 :    KUALA LUMPUR - GENTING HIGHLANDS - KUALA LUMPUR
   Proceed for full day visit to Genting Highlands. Enroute visit Batu Caves.Arrive Awana & board the One Way Cable Car to the Genting Highland. Upon arrival, enjoy the Indoor Theme Park or try your luck at casino. Evening back to Kuala Lumpur.Overnight at hotel in Kuala Lumpur.
Day 07 :    KUALA LUMPUR (Sunway Lagoon)
   Today full day visit to Sunway Lagoon - Water Park which is home to The Vuvuzela, the world’s largest water ride, the Waterplex 5D, the ultimate interactive cinematic experience, the FlowRider, Malaysia’s first surf simulator and many other great attractions. You can also Surf the Beach which is one of the world’s largest man-made beaches, it’s made up of over 13,000 square metres of sand where you can surf perfect waves, play volleyball or get a sun tan. Evening back. Overnight Kuala Lumpur.
Day 08 :    KUALA LUMPUR - SINGAPORE - CRUISE
   Check out & transfer to Singapore by Luxury coach. After 6 hrs journey arrive Singapore WTC (Harbor Front) to board the  Cruise. Cruise departs from Singapore at 2100 hrs .Overnight in Cruise.
Day 09 :    CRUISE
   Enjoy various activities on Cruise.Overnight in Cruise. (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner on Cruise)
Day 10 :    CRUISE - SINGAPORE
   Arrive Singapore at 1000 hrs. After Baggage Collection transfer to check in hotel. Overnight at hotel. .Evening Visit Night Safari. It is world’s first nocturnal zoo and renowned for its unique breeding and research methods. Witness varied species of animals in their natural environment and get close to them via joy tram ride passing through the night zoo. Overnight Singapore 
Day 11 :    SINGAPORE - SENTOSA ISLAND TOUR
   After Breakfast,visit for City Tour, which includes visit to The Singapore River, Botanical and Orchid Gardens, Parliament House and City Hall. Etc. Famous Sentosa Island. Take a Cable Car from Mt. Faber. Visit the Wax Museum. We visit to Under Water World etc. Then take Monorail ride across the Sentosa Island. In the evening enjoy The Musical Fountain show. Overnight in Singapore.
Day 12 :    SINGAPORE - UNIVERSAL STUDIO
   Proceed to visit Universal Studios.Among many thrilling attractions at Resorts World Sentosa is the region’s first and only Hollywood movie theme park. Whether they’re young or old, single or family-oriented, first-timers or seasoned travellers - there’s something for everyone among the park’s 24 rides and attractions based on Hollywood blockbusters, 18 of which are unique to the resort. Visitors will meet roving celebrities like Marilyn Monroe and Charlie Chaplin, visit the first castle from the world of Shrek, or ride the world’s tallest pair of dueling roller coasters. Overnight in Singapore.
Day 13 :    SINGAPORE - MUMBAI
   After taking breakfast today, it's time to check out of from the hotel and be transferred to the airport departing from Singapore to Mumbai. And to say good buy to all your new Destinations Visited and is time for you to board your flight back to home  We hope you return back to your home with wonderful memories of your Holiday with Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels.
