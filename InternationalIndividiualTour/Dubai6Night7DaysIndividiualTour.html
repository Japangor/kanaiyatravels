Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    Mumbai To Dubai, - On Arrival, Evening Enjoy Dhow Cruise With Dinner
   Assemble at Mumbai International Airport 3 hrs. prior to the departure of your Flight. Upon arrival at Dubai our Representative will meet and assist you to the hotel. Evening enjoy Dhow Cruise - a traditional wooden vessel and while sailing, witness the scale of the development of Dubai, also enjoy on board Dinner with music. Overnight at Hotel. (Dinner)
Day 02 :    Dubai City Tour + Monorail (One-Way) + Burj Khalifa 124th Floor (Non-Prime) + Dubai Mall + Dubai Fountain Show
   This Morning after Breakfast at the Hotel, Later proceed for City Orientation Tour of Dubai: You can enjoy the city tour by Viewing at the glance of Gold Souk: the traditional Gold Market in Dubai, Spice souk: The Traditional Spice Market in Dubai, Burj-Al-Arab: The Luxury 7 Star Hotel of Dubai (Photo Stop) and Jumeirah Beach: The white sand beach that is located and named after the Jumeirah district. Later proceed & enjoy the Monorail Ride (One-Way), With the View of Palm Atlantis: The Luxury 5 Star Hotel in Dubai. Proceed to Dubai Mall. Dubai Mall is the 2nd Largest Mall in the World. Rest free time at leisure at Dubai Mall. Visit Burj Khalifa. Burj Khalifa is the one of the biggest Skyscraper in Dubai & also referred as the “WORLD’S TALLEST BUILDING”. Enjoy the Non- Prime Time at the 124th Floor at the Burj Khalifa. Later in the evening enjoy The Dubai Fountain: The Light & Musical fountain Show. The Dubai Fountain is a choreographed fountain system located on the 12 hectare artificial Burj Khalifa Lake. Enjoy your Dinner at the Restaurant / Hotel. Nightstay at Dubai.. (Breakfast, Dinner)
Day 03 :    Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner. - (Breakfast, Dinner)
   This Morning after Breakfast at the Hotel, you can Just Chill, Relax. Later Proceed for the Desert Safari. Enjoy your evening at the Vast Arabian Deserts & the Rich experience of Sand Dunes & Activities: Sand Dune Bashing, Camel safari Ride, Heena Painting (Mehndi Art), Tea-Coffee & Juices, Hubble Bubble (Shisha), Belly Dancing, Fire Show, Sun-Set Photography & Tanoura dance Show & many more Activities. Later Enjoy the BBQ Dinner at Desert Safari. Nightstay at Dubai.
Day 04 :    Dubai – Full Day Abu Dhabi City Tour + Grand Mosque + Ferrari World (Photo-Stop) - (Breakfast, Dinner)
   his Morning after Breakfast at the Hotel & proceed for Abu Dhabi City Orientation Tour. Later Visit A Grand Mosque:  The Largest Mosque of the country, it is the key place of worship for daily prayers. You will also Visit the Ferrari World Abu Dhabi: The Theme Park of Abu Dhabi (Photo-Stop). Rules for Grand Mosque: No transparent Clothing for all. Female: Fully Covered body, hands till wrist and legs till Foot along with the Scarf for Fully Covered Hairs. Male: Elbow and Knees should be Covered. Later enjoy your Dinner at the Restaurant / Hotel. Nightstay at Dubai..
Day 05 :    Dubai - For Personal Activities + Tour in Limousine Ride (01 Hour)
   This Morning after Breakfast at Hotel, you can Just Chill, Relax, free time at Leisure or Shopping or you can enjoy the optional Sightseeing’s. Dubai by Night – The Panoramic Tour in Limousine Ride: The Lavish 1 Hour Limousine Ride during the Night time at city of Dubai & enjoy the Drive through Panoramic night view of Dubai. Nightstay at Dubai.. (Breakfast, Dinner)
Day 06 :    Combo: Miracle Garden & Global Village
   This Morning after Breakfast at the Hotel & Relax Free at Leisure.
Later in the afternoon proceed for The Miracle Garden:  The Miracle Garden is a flower garden located in Dubai. The garden was launched on Valentine's Day in 2013. It occupies over 72,000 Sq. Mtr. (780,000 sq. ft.), making it the world's largest natural flower garden, featuring over 50 million flowers and 250 million.  The Global Village: Global Village is located in Dubai. It combines cultures of 90 countries across the world at one place. It claims to be the world's largest tourism, leisure, shopping & entertainment project. It is the region's first cultural, entertainment, family and shopping destination. Overnight stay at Hotel in Dubai.. (Breakfast & Dinner)
Day 07 :    Dubai To Mumbai
   After Breakfast check out and enjoy last minute shopping for dates and chocolates. Check out at 10.30am from Hotel and transfer to the Airport to board your Flight back home. Do not forget to keep some funds for Dubai duty free shopping. We hope you return back with pleasant memories of your holiday with Shree Kanaiya Travels. (Breakfast)
