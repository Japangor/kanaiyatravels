Tourist Visa

  • 1 Form
  • 2 Photos (white b/g, 35x45mm in size, WITHOUT stapler marks, matt or semi matt with full facial
  • features visible. Maximum face portion to be covered on the photo.)
  • Student Bonafide certificate or I-D Card copy
  • Hotel confirmation voucher, directly from Switzerland
  • Forex or International Credit card
  • Covering letter explaining the reason of travel with name & designation of the signatory
  • Proof of income i.e. last 3 years I-T challan or TDS Certificate
  • Latest bank statement (For last 3 months only)
  • Salary Certificate
  • Overseas medical policy with repatriation clause & coverage of more than 30000 Euro
  • For Newly married couple, "Original Marriage Certificate", "duly attested" by the home department is a must, without which visa will not be issued.

Special Notes

  • If passenger is studying in a School/College, then the I-D card copy or any other photo proof is compulsory, failing which the consulate will not accept the case.
