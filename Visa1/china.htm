Tourist Visa :

  • 1 Form
  • 1 Photo (Passport size, white b/g, matt finish, full facial features visible clearly)
  • Air tickets with airlines stamp(showing sectors into mainland of China)
  • Covering letter addressed to the Consulate General of China, explaining the reason of travel with name & designation of the signatory
  • Bank Statement with a minimum closing balance of Rs. 1,50,000/- (Original + Copy)
  • Hotel confirmation voucher directly from China

Business Visa:

  • 1 Form
  • 1 Photo(Passport size, white b/g, matt finish, full facial features visible clearly)
  • Itinerary print out
  • Covering letter addressed to the Consulate General of China, explaining the reason of travel with name & designation of the signatory
  • Hotel confirmation voucher directly from China
  • Bank Statement with a minimum closing balance of Rs. 1,50,000/- (Original + Copy)
  • INVITATION fax addressed to the Consulate General of China, Mumbai, to be submitted along with the case. The Consulate will ask for the original Legal papers from the Ministry of External Affairs in China in case pax requires 1 year multiple entry visa.

Submission Timings: 0800 hrs to 1000 hrs.

Collection Timings:

0900 hrs to 1400 hrs. ( Monday - friday)
