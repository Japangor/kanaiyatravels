Tourist Visa :

  • 1 Application form duly filled & signed
  • 2 passport size photographs in white background.
  • Covering letter
  • foreign currency endorsement or copy of international credit card
  • Hotel reservations covering the entire duration of stay.
  • Confirmed return air tickets/ booking print out with agency stamp

Business Visa:

  • 1 Application form duly filled & signed
  • 2 passport size photographs in white background.
  • Covering letter
  • foreign currency endorsement or copy of international credit card
  • Hotel reservations covering the entire duration of stay.
  • Confirmed return air tickets/ booking print out with agency stamp
  • A copy of invitation fax directly from Kenya to be submitted along with the case.
  • Relevant Business Correspondence from Kenya

Submission Timings: 1100 hrs to 1330 hrs.

Collection Timings:

5 working days (Cases are sent to New Delhi)

Fees :

Rs.2200/- for Tourist and business payable by demand draft in the name of "Kenya High Commission" payable at New Delhi.
