Tourist Visa
  • One Application form
  • Two Recent passport size (35x40mm, face should be 80% of the photo area, white b/g only)
  • Return confirmed air tickets
  • Proof of accommodation i.e. hotel reservation directly from a hotel in Austria.
  • Overseas medical insurance valid for all Schengen-countries with a minimum coverage of 30,000 EURO (IN ORIGINAL)
  • Covering letter in details highlighting the reason of travel, Proof of your current occupation, A no objection letter from your company stating your leave.
  • Proof of Sufficient funds means for your stay (recent (3 month) bank statement with the telephone number of the bank clearly visible.
    Note: Credit cards and cash are not accepted as proof of financial means.
    Note: Credit card statements may be submitted as additional supporting documentation.
  • Proof of income i.e. last 3 years I-T Challan or TDS Certificate
  • Salary Certificate (last 3 months)
  • Incase of a minor traveling alone a no objection letter from both the parents attested by a notary. Incase of a minor traveling with one of the parent, a no objection letter from the other spouse attested by a notary is to be provided.
