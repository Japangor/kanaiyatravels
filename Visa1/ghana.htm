Tourist Visa :

  • 4 Forms
  • 4 Photos
  • Confirm return Air tickets
  • Covering letter explaining the reason of travel, exact nature of business in India with name & designation of the signatory and that of the pax who is traveling.
  • Two references from Ghana required with their complete name and address
  • Proof of income i.e. last 3 years I-T Challan or TDS Certificate
  • Latest bank statement
  • Salary Certificate
  • Yellow Fever Vaccination
  • Birth Certificate of the child traveling with parent(s)

Business Visa:

  • 4 Forms
  • 4 Photos
  • Return confirmed Air tickets
  • Correspondence with the company in Ghana or any other document in support.
  • Covering letter explaining the reason of travel with name & designation of the signatory and that of the pax who is traveling.
  • Two references from Ghana required with their complete name and address
  • Proof of income i.e. last 3 years I-T Challan or TDS Certificate
  • Latest bank statement
  • Salary Certificate

Submission Timings: 1030 hrs to 1300 hrs.

Collection Timings:

1400 hrs to 1500 hrs (Third day)

Fees :

Rs. 2700/- (Single Entry) & Rs. 5400/- (Multiple Entry)

Special Notes:

  • Plus INVITATION FAX directly to the consulate and 1 to be submitted along with the case with an undertaking that the host will ensure that the visitor complies fully with the immigration laws of Ghana.
