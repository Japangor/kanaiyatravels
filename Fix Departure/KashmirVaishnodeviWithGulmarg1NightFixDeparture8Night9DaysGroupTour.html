Days Tour Detail
Day 01 :    ARRIVE SRINAGAR (20 KM / 1 Hrs) ) OR
JAMMU / UDHAMPUR TO SRINAGAR ( 280 KM / 10 Hrs & Minimum 6 Passanger Req)
   Tour participants are requested to contact the Manager/ Driver of Shree Kanaiya Travels on arrival at Srinagar Airport. Transfar the Srinagar Hotel / Houseboat. Evening free time for Shikara ride Or Rest Or Shopping. Nighthalt at Srinagar Hotel / Houseboat. OR Passengers coming to Jammu by train will be reach srinagar by shared vehicle from Jammu Station.. (Minimum 6 Person Req.)
Day 02 :    SRINAGAR - SONMARG - SRINAGAR (170 KM RETURN)
   After breakfast full day trip to Sonmarg, which is located at 85 kms from Srinagar Sonmarg means meadow of gold Sonmarg is the home of many beautiful & serene lakes. Enjoy sledge car ride in this beautiful snow point. Also can Inrested are Visit Snow Point at Zozila pass (Subject to Military Permission) OR Thajiwas By Local Texi .(at your own cost), Evening return back to Srinagar. Enroute Enjoy River Rafting & visit Khir Bhavani Temple. Nighthalt at Srinagar.
Day 03 :    SRINAGAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING & VISIT DUDHPATHRI OR YUSHMARG (OPTIONAL & AT YOUR OWN RISK - HORSE RIDING POINT -- ONLY IF THE SITUATION IN KASHMIR IS 100% SAFE & GOOD )
   Early & after breakfast, we will drive to Dudhpathri OR Yushmarg (100 km return & horse riding point). Upon arrival take a pony on rent to visit other places & enjoy photography. (only if the condition of Kashmir is 100% good and safe we will travel to Yushmarg or Dudhpathri). Afternon Back Srinagar & After Lunch, we will be visit Moghul Gardens (i.e. t Baug - Chashmeshahi Garden - Tulip Garden and Shalimar Garden). Overnightstay at Srinagar. 
Day 04 :    SRINAGAR TO GULMARG (65 KM / 2 Hrs)
   After Breakfast proceed for Gulmarg (2 Hrs) Enroute pick up overcoats, gloves and boots, available on hire (optional) at Tangmarg. Arrive Gulmarg and transfer to Hotels. After Lunch walk upto Gandola Cable Car Station (1.5 Km from Hotels) to enjoy Gandola Cable Ride, Enjoy snow at this beautiful snow point. Evening Enjoy Horse Riding. Nighthalt at Gulmarg.
Day 05 :    GULMARG TO PAHELGAM (135 KM / 4 Hrs)
   After breakfast proceed to Pahalgam (140 km / 4 - 5 hrs) situated at an altitude of 7,200 ft above sea level on the banks of Lidder River, also known as the most beautiful hill station of India. Take a tour of the Saffron Firm and Cricket Bat Factory on the way. Reach Pahalgam and transfer to hotel. In the evening enjoy photography of natural beauty in the heaven on earth. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.
Day 06 :    PAHELGAM
   After breakfast enjoy natural beauty at Chandanwadi & Betaab Valley (Include In Tour Cost) the famous shooting point of Kashmir Valley. After Lunch Visit Kashmir Vally, Dabyan Valley, Baisaran Vally By horse riding (at Your Own Cost) at this beautiful place, Evening is free time for shopping in Pahelgam market. Night halt at Pahelgam.
Day 07 :    PAHELGAM TO KATRA (260 KM / 8 Hrs)
   Early & After breakfast we proceed to katra (Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Base Camp - 260 km & 8 hrs Journey), Arrive at Late Evening. After Dinner this night can intrested are start for Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Yatra by Horse (Rs. 1800/- ), Dolly (Rs. 4000/-) OR Walk(Free) (Return 28 km), Also avaliable Helicopter Sarvice for Darshan (Only Day Time), Helicopter droped 3 km before Temple, Book Your Helicopter Ticket Directly from (https://www.maavaishnodevi.org). Nighthalt at Katra.
Day 08 :    KATRA (Full Day Free time for Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan)
   Full Day For Shree Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan..Evening Free time for Rest OR Shopping in Katra Market. Nighthalt at Katra.. 
Day 09 :    DROP TO KATRA RAILWAY STATION (3 km From Hotel) 
   In the morning after breakfast at 8.30am you will be dropped at Katra / Jammu railway station by shared vehicle to board the train. The tour ends with a memorable tour experience organized by Shree Kanaiya Tours & Travels. Passanger traveling By Air from Jammu will have to reach Jammu at their own expense.
